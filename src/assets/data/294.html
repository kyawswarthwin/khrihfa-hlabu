KAN PATHIAN A RA LIO

Doh is C

1. Bawipa a ratnak sunparnak kha ka mit nih a hmuh cang Thin-hunnak mitsur thei an khonnak hmun kha a naan dih cang Tih a nungmi a vainam har nim-tlau kha a chuahter cang, Kan Pathian a ra lio.

CHO: Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Kan Pathian a ra lio.

2. Riah-nak hmun mei ceu tampi lakah Amah cu ka hmuh i Khi ka laifang ah cun Amah caah biaktheng kha an ser; A ceu tuk lo meiinn lak hmanh ah a dinnak ka hmuh khawh, Kan Pathian a ra lio.

CHO: Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Kan Pathian a ra lio.

3. Muko a tum cang i kir than u, a kan ti bal lai lo Amah biaceih thutdan hmai ah mi lungthin kha a vamh hna Maw ka thlarau Amah cu let law ka ke hna i lawm u, Kan Pathian a ra lio.

CHO: Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Kan Pathian a ra lio.

4. Lily pangpar dawh bangin ram hlapi ah Jesuh a chuak, amah sunparnak nih nang le kei zong kha a kan dawhter; Khamh awkah a thih bang mi luatter awkah thi ve u si, Kan Pathian a ra lio.

CHO: Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Lawm u, Lawm u, Hal-le-lu-jah! Kan Pathian a ra lio.
