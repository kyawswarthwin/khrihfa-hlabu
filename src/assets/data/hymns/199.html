MAW DINHNAK LE LAWMHNAK NI

Doh is F

1. Maw dinhnak le lawmhnak ni, Thangthat le ceunak ni, Ngaihchiat khuaruahhar damnak, Aa dawh bik, a ceu bik Bawipa nih thihnak tei in, Thlarau tum ni a si, "Thiang bik, thiang bik, thiang bik" tiah, Na thangthat hla an sa.

2. Maw dinhnak le lawmhnak ni, Ceunak hramthawk na si, Khamhtu Jesuh a thawh ni, Kannih lawmh ni na si, Bawipa nih thihnak tei in, Thlarau tum ni a si, Mah ni sunglawi ni ah cun, Ceu phun thum a kan pek.

3. Thlarau rawltam mi ca-ah, Van manna a surter, A thiangmi pumhnak hmun ah, Muko in a auh hna; Ni bang Pathian Thawngthabia, A ceunak hmun kip ah, Nunnak tiva a luang i, Thlarau zong a nunter.

4. Dinhnak le lawmhnak nihin, Thluachuahnak thar kan hmun, Zarh thar kan luhmi chungah, Hi lawmhnak a dong lo, Dinhnak le lawmhnak ni thar, A hung chuah than tiangin, Pa, Fapa le Thiang Thlarau, Thangthat in um ko seh.
