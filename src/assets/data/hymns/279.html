JESUH LAWNG NIH MAW VAILAM A PUT LAI?

Doh is A

1. Hi vawlei ah Jesuh lawng nih, Vailam a put lai maw? Mikip ca put awk a um ko, Keica zong a um ve.

2. Vailamtahnak cu ka put lai, Thihnak caan ka phak tiang, Cun Bawi luchin cu kaa chinh lai, Ka inn ka phak tikah.

3. An khenhmi Jesuh ke-hram ah, Thlalang lung phah cungah, Ka sui luchin cu ka chiah lai, A min ka thangthat lai.

4. Maw vailam sung! Maw sui luchin! A lianngan thawhthan ni, Vancungmi hna rung tum tuah u, Ka thlarau kai pi u.
