MI KIP CA BAIBAL THIANG

Doh is Bb

1. Mi vialte nih Baibal ca rel, Kan i tinhmi si cio ko seh, Khuaza kip ah a than hlan tiang, Thli nih kalpi lengmang ko seh.

CHO: Rili le fing tlang vialte ah, Pathian bia sung rung ceu ko tuah, Zan mui lak ah a vak mi hna, Pathian sin ah an phak hlan tiang.

2. A phaknak kipah a ceunak, Ni bangin a tlang zungzal ko, Sual riantuannak kha a tei i, Muihnak khuadawm kha a tian dih.

CHO: Rili le fing tlang vialte ah, Pathian bia sung rung ceu ko tuah, Zan mui lak ah a vak mi hna, Pathian sin ah an phak hlan tiang.

3. Kan Pa Pathian hmai a langhter, Ngaihthiam dawtnak he a ceu ko, Adam phun tlau ciami sinah, Van hnangamnak bia kha a chim.

CHO: Rili le fing tlang vialte ah, Pathian bia sung rung ceu ko tuah, Zan mui lak ah a vak mi hna, Pathian sin ah an phak hlan tiang.

4. Lung retheimi a dinhter hna, Ngaihchiami an hna a ngamter, Lungdermi thazang a thawnter, Zungzal ruahchannak a pek hna.

CHO: Rili le fing tlang vialte ah, Pathian bia sung rung ceu ko tuah, Zan mui lak ah a vak mi hna, Pathian sin ah an phak hlan tiang.
