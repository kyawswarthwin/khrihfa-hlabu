NA CHUNGAH KA THUP

Doh is F

1. Ngaihchiat le sifahnak ka in lioah khan, Ka thlarau Jesuh sinah zuan a duh ta, Kei sual retheimi na fa si ka duh ko, A zungzal Pathian keimah ka huppheng tuah

CHO: Nangmah chungah, ka huppheng tuah, Azungzal Pathian keimah ka huppheng tuah.

2. Chun hnangamnak le zan ngaihchiatnak lakah, Tukforhnak a tampi ka in lioah khan, Hi vawlei nun chung tilet tho lakah hin, A zungzal Pathian keimah ka huppheng tuah.

CHO: Nangmah chungah, ka huppheng tuah, Azungzal Pathian keimah ka huppheng tuah.

3. Ral he kan i doh caan a tampi tikah, A ka hupphengtu sin ka zoh i ka chimh, Hneksaknak rili chung tilet thawh tikah, Jesuh, ka thlarau hngatchannak nang na si.

CHO: Nangmah chungah, ka huppheng tuah, Azungzal Pathian keimah ka huppheng tuah.
