NUPI THA

Doh is G

1. Nupi tha cu hlawn nakin a sunglawi deuh, A va cu a si a fahter bal lai lo, A nun chung a va ca thatnak lawng a tuah, bochan awk tlak nupi tha a si.

CHO: A fale nih khan thluachuahnak nu tiah an ti, A va zong nih a thang-thatnak lawng a chim "Nupi tha tampi an um si hmanh seh law, Nang tluk an um lo ka dawt," a ti, Iang hi hlen a hmang i dawh hi a ziam kho, Bawipa a tihmi nu cu thangthat u, A kut theitlai ruangah khan thangthat u, A tuanmi ruangah upat pe u, pe.
