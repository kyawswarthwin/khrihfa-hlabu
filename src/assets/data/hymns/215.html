BAWIPA AUHNAK

Doh is Ab

1. Bawipa nih a facang lo chungah, Riantuan awk a kan auh, Facang zuntu kha an tlawm ca-ah, An caan kha a luan cang.

CHO: Lunglawm in Bawipa thawngin chuak tuah, Dawtnak velngeihnak he, Khamhnak laksawng thenh awk tha bik kha, Mi vialte hngalhter hna.

2. Sifakmi hna kha lawmhnak chungah, Zaangfah rawldanghnak ah, Bawipa nunnak zungzal co awkah, Annih cu run hruai hna.

CHO: Lunglawm in Bawipa thawngin chuak tuah, Dawtnak velngeihnak he, Khamhnak laksawng thenh awk tha bik kha, Mi vialte hngalhter hna.

3. Atu thawk in tuan hramthawk cang tuah, A ceu a dih zau lai, A donghnak caan kha a tlun tikah, Kan tuan kho ti lai lo.

CHO: Lunglawm in Bawipa thawngin chuak tuah, Dawtnak velngeihnak he, Khamhnak laksawng thenh awk tha bik kha, Mi vialte hngalhter hna.
