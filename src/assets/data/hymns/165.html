BAWIPA CU A TUM THAN LAI

Doh is G

1. Bawipa cu a rung tum than lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Sunparnak in a rung tum lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Amah cu dinnak Bawi a si, A tlanhmi thluachuah a pek lai, Bawi thutdan ah bia a ceih lai, Timh cia in um tuah u.

CHO: Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Zing, chun le zan caan zongah, Mei inn zinan rawn cia in, Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim cang maw?

2. Bawipa cu a tum than zau lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Amah zummi a hruai hna lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Vancung darkhing kha an tum lai, Bawipa khamhnak a tlun tikah, Nan meiinn tha tein vang law, Timh cia in um tuah u.

CHO: Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Zing, chun le zan caan zongah, Mei inn zinan rawn cia in, Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim cang maw?

3. Bawipa nih bia a ceih dih lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Muko auhnak thawng kan theih lai, Timh cia in um tuah u, Tlangcung le ramkip mi vialte, Biaceih awkah a kan auh lai, Amah hmaiah kan dir cio lai, Timh cia in um tuah u.

CHO: Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim lai maw? Zing, chun le zan caan zongah, Mei inn zinan rawn cia in, Nu-pi-thi rat tikah naa tim cang maw?
