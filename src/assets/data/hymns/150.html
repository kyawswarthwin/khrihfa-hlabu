GALILEE PHILHLONAK

Doh is A

1. Galilee ah Bawipa Jesuh, Cawnpiak pah in, A umkal tawn, Vancung kong cawnpiaknak caah, Tahchunhnak kha a hman tawn hna.

CHO: Maw, Galilee ram thluachuakmi Jesuh a chung ah a leng tawn, Kan ram zong nih Jesuh a herh, A chungah leng ve seh kan ti.

2. Pangpar phunkip tlimno vialte, Lei le van thil, Vialte hna nih, Pathian thatnak a sunparning, A thiannak he an kan chimh fawn.

CHO: Maw, Galilee ram thluachuakmi Jesuh a chung ah a leng tawn, Kan ram zong nih Jesuh a herh, A chungah leng ve seh kan ti.

3. Fiang tein hmu kho u si law, Vawlei dihlak, Biakinn a si, Sermi thil vialte hna zong hi, Pathian lamkal tu an si dih.

CHO: Maw, Galilee ram thluachuakmi Jesuh a chung ah a leng tawn, Kan ram zong nih Jesuh a herh, A chungah leng ve seh kan ti.
