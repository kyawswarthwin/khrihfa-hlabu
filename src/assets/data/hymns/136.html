THI SUNG

Doh is Eb

1. Bawi Jesuh thi a chuakmi cu, Keimah ca a sung bik, Tihnak le sualpalhnak chungin, Ka thlarau aa din cang.

CHO: Maw khuaruahhar tiva a luang, A kan Khamhbawi chungin, Van phak tiangin ka hla tha cu, "Thi sung thletmi" a si.

2. "An sualnak le palhnak vialte, Ka ngaihthiam cang hna lai, Annih caah ka Fa a thi," tiah Pathian nih a ti.

CHO: Maw khuaruahhar tiva a luang, A kan Khamhbawi chungin, Van phak tiangin ka hla tha cu, "Thi sung thletmi" a si.

3. Philh khawh lomi ka sualnak nih, A ka tihter lai lo, A sung thi a chuakmi thawng in, Ngaihthiamnak ka hmuh cang.

CHO: Maw khuaruahhar tiva a luang, A kan Khamhbawi chungin, Van phak tiangin ka hla tha cu, "Thi sung thletmi" a si.

4. Zaang a dermi ka pumsa nih, Zawtnak kha tong hmanhseh, Khi thi chungah kaa dinh ahcun, Lungdai in ka thi lai.

CHO: Maw khuaruahhar tiva a luang, A kan Khamhbawi chungin, Van phak tiangin ka hla tha cu, "Thi sung thletmi" a si.
