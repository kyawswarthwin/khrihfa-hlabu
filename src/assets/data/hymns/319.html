VANCUNGMI NIH AN LAK

Doh is Ab

1. Mirum pa innka hram pawngah a thu i, La-za-ruh cu a thi dengmang i, Vawlei sifah re-theihnak kha a in cang, A thih hnuah lunglawmhnak a hmuh.

CHO: Lawmhnak chungah khan vancungmi nih an lak, Vancung khua ah hlasak thawng a khat, Vancung sunparnak ah Khamhbawi nih a lak, A dawtmi hna sinah a kai cang.

2. Vawlei sifah in hnu ah kan thih ahcun, Kannih vialte kan lung aa lawm lai, Vawlei cung nunnak kha thilrit phorh bantuk, Ngaih-chiatnak mitthli tlaknak a si.

CHO: Lawmhnak chungah khan vancungmi nih an lak, Vancung khua ah hlasak thawng a khat, Vancung sunparnak ah Khamhbawi nih a lak, A dawtmi hna sinah a kai cang.

3. Jesuh hnu zulmi nih liam hmanh hna sehlaw, A sungmi hlawn vancung khua a um, Tukforh awlok-chonnak kha kan kal tak lai, Thlennak caan ah kan lung a lawm lai.

CHO: Lawmhnak chungah khan vancungmi nih an lak, Vancung khua ah hlasak thawng a khat, Vancung sunparnak ah Khamhbawi nih a lak, A dawtmi hna sinah a kai cang.

4. Pathian umnak leiah hmaiah fong chin tuah, Na fimnak kha Jesuh ca hmang tuah, Na tuannak tlawm hmanhseh, laksawng a tam lai, Vailam put in luchin naa chinh lai.
