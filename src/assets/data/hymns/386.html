AA ZUAMMI KHRIHFA BU

Doh is C

1. A hlat a nai, Khrihfabu-mi Pumh awkah ra chuak cio tuah u, Ruah-channak le lung-lawmhnak in Pathian bia kha ngai cio tuah u,

CHO: Aa zuammi Khrihfabu, Jesuh sin aa pummi, Lungkhat thinkhat tein, A min liannak chim u; Pathian pennak caah, Zuam cio ko tuah u si; Kan Khrihfabu hmunhnak thlacam zungzal u si.

2. Kan Siangpahrang Thangthatnak hla Sak awk caah, kan i pum i Mino zong nih Pathian sinah, Zungzal lu-nunnak kha an hmuh,

CHO: Aa zuammi Khrihfabu, Jesuh sin aa pummi, Lungkhat thinkhat tein, A min liannak chim u; Pathian pennak caah, Zuam cio ko tuah u si; Kan Khrihfabu hmunhnak thlacam zungzal u si.

3. Kan Bawi riantuan, I tlaih ko u Kan biakamnak kha remh than u, Amah sin vancung phak hlan tiang, Zumh karhnak in rian tuan u si.

CHO: Aa zuammi Khrihfabu, Jesuh sin aa pummi, Lungkhat thinkhat tein, A min liannak chim u; Pathian pennak caah, Zuam cio ko tuah u si; Kan Khrihfabu hmunhnak thlacam zungzal u si.
