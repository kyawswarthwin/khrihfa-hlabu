NGAKCHIA ZONG NIH AN AUPI LAI

Doh is D

1. Ngakchuan semmawng hmanh nih tlanhnak kong an aupi lai, Khamhtu le a vailam tung put in a kan tlanhnak kong, Rili ral um khua zakip mi lung hna-ngamternak ca, An thawhlawm in Pathian Baibal ca cu an kuat hna.

CHO: Ngakchuan semmawng hih lunglawmhnak thawng an thanh lai, Misung Khamhtu a dawtnak, Ngakchuan semmawng nih an aupi lai i, Vancung lam cu an hmuhsak hna lai.

2. Ngakchuan semmawng hmanh nih Bawipa rian an tuan ve lai, Ngaih-chiatnak khuadawm le minmei cu an tianter khawh lai, Lung rethei le thadi zaangbang ngaihchia lungkuai mi kha, Niceu dawh bang hnemhnak an-nem in an hnemh khawh hna.

CHO: Ngakchuan semmawng hih lunglawmhnak thawng an thanh lai, Misung Khamhtu a dawtnak, Ngakchuan semmawng nih an aupi lai i, Vancung lam cu an hmuhsak hna lai.

3. Semmawng rual cu Khamhtu nih a hnakkar ah a tenh hna, Velngeih thluachuah khamhnak cu an hrawm khawh ve hna lai i, Dawtnak in a zohkhenh hna kha bia in a fianter, Zohkhenhtu vanmi Pathian nih a chiahpiak hna a ti.

CHO: Ngakchuan semmawng hih lunglawmhnak thawng an thanh lai, Misung Khamhtu a dawtnak, Ngakchuan semmawng nih an aupi lai i, Vancung lam cu an hmuhsak hna lai.
