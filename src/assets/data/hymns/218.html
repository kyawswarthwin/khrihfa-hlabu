THANGTHATNAK A UM HNGA MAW?

Doh is A

1. Vancung a tha a ceu khua khi ka ruat ni-hin, Ni tlak tik ka tlunnak ding khua khi, Khamhtu dawtnak thawngin khi ram ka phak tikah, Sunparnak kei ca a um hnga maw?

CHO: Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? Vawlei cung ka nunnak ni a liam tikah cun, (tikah cun) Zungzal kaa dinhnak inn tha ka phak khawh tikah, Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? (um hnga maw)

2. Ka tuan khawh tiang Bawipa hngatchan in ka tuan lai, Thlarau tlaitu ah run ka ser tuah, A-mah sunparnak hmaiah khin ka dir tikah, Thangthat kha kei ca a si nakhnga.

CHO: Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? Vawlei cung ka nunnak ni a liam tikah cun, (tikah cun) Zungzal kaa dinhnak inn tha ka phak khawh tikah, Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? (um hnga maw)

3. Bawipa ka hmuh tikah ka lunglawmhnak vialte, Zeitluk in dah ka chim khawh te hnga, Aa dawh le a ceu sui khua tha ka phak tikah, Thangthat le sunpar kei ca si cang.

CHO: Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? Vawlei cung ka nunnak ni a liam tikah cun, (tikah cun) Zungzal kaa dinhnak inn tha ka phak khawh tikah, Thangthatnak kei ca a um hnga maw? (um hnga maw)
