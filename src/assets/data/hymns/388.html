BAWIPA KHA ZUL KO

Doh is Db

1. Zei thil paoh tlung hmanhseh, Bawipa kha zul ko, Lam kha mui hmanhsehlaw, Hruaitu kha zul ko, Ral nih an in lem i, Tih awk nai hmanhseh, Jesuh sin kal ahcun tih awk a um lo,

CHO: Raltha in (Ral tha in, hmailei ah kal tuah), hmailei ah kal tuah, Na thazang (Na thazang thar chin ko seh; chin ko seh), thar chin ko seh, Nifa in na Khamhtu zul tuah, Amah nih a dongh tiang an hruai lai.

2. Khuazei na kal hmanh ah, Bawipa kha zul ko, Muihnak le harnak ah, Lam an hmuhsak lai, Amah ceunak nih na lam a ceuter lai, Thetse ram vialte kha a parter dih lai

CHO: Raltha in (Ral tha in, hmailei ah kal tuah), hmailei ah kal tuah, Na thazang (Na thazang thar chin ko seh; chin ko seh), thar chin ko seh, Nifa in na Khamhtu zul tuah, Amah nih a dongh tiang an hruai lai.

3. Lam ah i din loin, Bawipa kha zul ko, An fialmi nawl paoh cu, Khualrang in tuah ko, An fial tikah tho law, hmai leiah kal tuah, Jesuh he kal ahcun na tlau kho lai lo,

CHO: Raltha in (Ral tha in, hmailei ah kal tuah), hmailei ah kal tuah, Na thazang (Na thazang thar chin ko seh; chin ko seh), thar chin ko seh, Nifa in na Khamhtu zul tuah, Amah nih a dongh tiang an hruai lai.
