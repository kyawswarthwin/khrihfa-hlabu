NGAIHMANH! VANMI AUTHAWNG

Doh is G

1. Ngaihmanh, vanmi authawng cu, "Bawi hrinthar thangthat ko u, Leicung ah daihnak um seh, Pathian le mi an i rem, Miphun vialte hna dir u, Vanmi he hlasa ti u. Bethlehem ah Jesuh Khrih, Kan Khamhtu cu a chuak cang. Ngaihmanh! vanmi authawng cu, "Bawi hrinthar thangthat ko u."

2. Vancungmi lak Siangpahrang, A zungzal a nung kan Bawi, A caan a tlin in a tum, Lengnu tak ah a rung lang. Vawlei mi bang kan Bawi nih, Mi kha khamh a kan duh ca. A thiangmi a rung tum cang, Jesuh kan Emmanuel, Ngaihmanh! vanmi authawng cu, "Bawi hrinthar thangthat ko u."

3. Daihnak Bawi, kan Siangpahrang, Dinnak Bawi thangthat ko u, Ceunak, nunnak mi vialte, Pek awkah a rung tum cang. A sunparnak kaltak in, Thihnak in nunnak thar ah. Vawlei cung mi vialte ca Hrinnak kha a in kan Bawi, Ngaihmanh! vanmi authawng cu, "Bawi hrinthar thangthat ko u."
