A RAWKMI HLAWM TUAH

Doh is Bb

1. A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Thi deng zohkhenh tuah, Sualnak thlan in zaangfah in hung chuak tuah, A palhmi kha hram tuah, A rilmi tung tuah; Jesuh khamh thiamnak kong kha va chim tuah.

CHO: A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Thi deng zohkhenh tuah, Jesuh tlaih-channak nih, An khamh hna lai.

2. Sualnak ngaihchih-mi fa, A ngan ko rih hna, Amah bia zeiah an rel lo nain, Lungtak in va sawm tuah, Nem tein sawm tuah, An zumh ahcun ngaih-thiamnak an hmuh lai.

CHO: A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Thi deng zohkhenh tuah, Jesuh tlaih-channak nih, An khamh hna lai.

3. Mi lungthin chungah khan, Rul-hreu a hrolh ca, Thihnak le rawhnak nih khan a uk cang, Sihmanhseh dawtnak in, Na tongh hna ahcun, A rawk cia lung kha a nung than kho lai.

CHO: A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Thi deng zohkhenh tuah, Jesuh tlaih-channak nih, An khamh hna lai.

4. A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Tuan awk rian a si, Jesuh nih thazang fehnak an pek lai, Dawtnak in kirter tuah, A bi lam chungah, An nih ca Khamhtu a thih kha chim hna.

CHO: A rawkmi kha hlawm tuah, Thi deng zohkhenh tuah, Jesuh tlaih-channak nih, An khamh hna lai.
