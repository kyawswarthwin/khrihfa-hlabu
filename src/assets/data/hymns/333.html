JERUSALEM SUIKHUA KHA

Doh is D

1. Je-ru-sa-lem suikhua kha! Cawhnuk le khuai-hli ti, In a khat, Nangmah kong kha ka ruat dih kho lai lo, Na kong kha ka hngal kho lo, Lunglawm zeizat an um, Na lianh-ngannak zei bia dah! Nuamhnak ka chim kho lo.

2. Zion khua chungah an dir, Hlasaknak in a khat, Vancungmi he an cer ko, Mi-thiangmi zong an um; An siangpahrang he an um, Riantuannak um ti lo, Khamhnak a hmu mi umnak, Ni a tlang zungzal ko.

3. Hmaisa mi kan zulh ahcun, Sualnak ral kan tei lai, Zungzal hna-ngamnak khua tha, Kannih zong kan phan lai; Khi khua chungah ngaih-chiatnak, Zeihmanh a um lai lo, A thiannak zong kha a um, Zungzal a dong lai lo.

4. A nuambik vancung khua kha, Khamhnak a hmumi khua, Thluachuah vanluh umnak khua, Kannih nih phak kan duh, Maw nun thabik kan Bawipa, Ram tha chungah a um, Khi ram ah kan phak tikah, Amah he kan din lai.
