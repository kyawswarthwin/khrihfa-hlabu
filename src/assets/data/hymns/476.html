LAWMHNAK DARKHING THAWNG A THANG LAI

Doh is Ab

1. Na lung nih lawmhnak darkhing thawng a theih lai, Dinhnak na sin in a then bal ti lai lo, Kutka bi lei tu panh law, Jesuh ca nifa nung tuah, Na lung ah lawmhnak darkhing thawng a thang lai

CHO: Lawmhnak darkhing na theih lai, (darkhing na theih lai), Lawmhnak darkhing na theih lai. Khuazei ka na kal hmanh ah, Khamhtu min kha i lak law, Na lung ah lawmhnak darkhing thawng na theih lai.

2. A tlingmi Jesuh dawtnak cu na hngalh lai, Hi dawtnak cu na velchum ah a lang lai, Dawtnak bianem chim zungzal, Vel he nifa riantuan tuah, Na lung ah lawmhnak darkhing thawng a thang lai.

3. Na kalnk van lam ah harnak um hmanhseh, A vel cu tam chin in chin in na sin an pek lai, Mit hmuh lo a si nain, Na sin a um an naih ko, Na lung ah lawmhnak darkhing thawng a thang lai.

4. Na nunnak cu Jesuh kong chim in hmang tuah, A dinnak cu ngei law mi va phaw ve hna, Nunnak thiang na ngeih ahcun, misual na bawmh khawh hna lai Na lung-lawmhnak darkhing thawng a thang lai.
