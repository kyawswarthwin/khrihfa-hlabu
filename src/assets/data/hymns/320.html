RUAHCHANNAK LUNGPI

Doh is G

1. Ka ruahchannak a hram a fek, Jesuh Khrih thi lawng in a si, Ka bochan dang a um ti lo, Jesuh a min lawng in ka tei.

CHO: Lungfek in Jesuh ka bochan, A dang bochan awk a um lo, A dang bochan awk a um lo.

2. A mithmai ceu mui nih a phenh, A zaangfahnak kaa bochan lai, Thlichia hrang tilet tho hmanhseh, Ka hngatchannak Bawipa a si.

CHO: Lungfek in Jesuh ka bochan, A dang bochan awk a um lo, A dang bochan awk a um lo.

3. A biakam bangin a thi cang, Phannak kip ah kaa bochan lai, Ka velchum vial a rawh tikah Ka bochannak Jesuh Bawipa.

CHO: Lungfek in Jesuh ka bochan, A dang bochan awk a um lo, A dang bochan awk a um lo.

4. Muko thawng he a tum tikah, A hmaika ah ka dir te lai; A dinnak in kaa thuamh tikah, Sualnak loin ka um te lai.

CHO: Lungfek in Jesuh ka bochan, A dang bochan awk a um lo, A dang bochan awk a um lo.
