JESUH BAN CHUNGAH HIM IN

Doh is G

1. Jesuh ban chungah him in, Amah tang ah ka um, Hika hin a dawtnak ca, Lung-daihnak in ka um, Ngai u law, vancung mi nih, Hi bia kha hla an sak, Thihnak ti khatte lei ah, Vancung khua ah an sak.

CHO: Jesuh ban chungah him in, Amah tang ah ka um. Hika hin a dawtnak ca, Lung daihnak in ka um.

2. Jesuh ban chungah him in, Tukforh in ka him ko, Thinphan lunghno a dai lai, Sualnak in ka rawk lo, Mitthli tlaknak kha a tlawm, Sifahnak tlawm pal um rih, Ngaihchiat hna-hnawhnak zong kha, A dih dengmang ko cang.

CHO: Jesuh ban chungah him in, Amah tang ah ka um. Hika hin a dawtnak ca, Lung daihnak in ka um.

3. Keimah ca Bawi Jesuh nih, A sungmi lu a hloh, A hmun zungzal Pathian kha, Keimah ka hngatchan ko, Ka thih caan a phak hlan tiang, Lungthat in um ko ning, Deirel a chuah hlan tiang in, Lung that in um ko ning.

CHO: Jesuh ban chungah him in, Amah tang ah ka um. Hika hin a dawtnak ca, Lung daihnak in ka um.
