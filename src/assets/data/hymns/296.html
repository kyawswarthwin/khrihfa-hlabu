JESUH ZUMH CU A NUAM

Doh is Ab

1. Jesuh zumh cu a tha bik ko, A bia bang in zumh a nuam, A biakam chungah i dinh le hngalh cu, Bawipa nih a duh.

CHO: Jesuh, Jesuh, ka zumhning cu, Lungtak in ka ruat than tawn, Jesuh, Jesuh, mi sung Jesuh, A vel ruangah ka zumh chin.

2. Jesuh zumh cu a tha hringhran, A thi in a ka tawlnak, Damnak ti thiang ah a ka hnim, Ka zumhnak a tlawm na-in.

CHO: Jesuh, Jesuh, ka zumhning cu, Lungtak in ka ruat than tawn, Jesuh, Jesuh, mi sung Jesuh, A vel ruangah ka zumh chin.

3. Jesuh zumh cu a tha taktak, Sual lung-puamnak cu a hloh, Jesuh nih a dawtnak ruangah, Zungzal a ka umpi lai.

CHO: Jesuh, Jesuh, ka zumhning cu, Lungtak in ka ruat than tawn, Jesuh, Jesuh, mi sung Jesuh, A vel ruangah ka zumh chin.

4. Ka zumh ca ka lung a lawm khun, Misung Jesuh hawitha bik, Ka sinah a um ti ka hngalh, Zungzal a ka umpi lai.

CHO: Jesuh, Jesuh, ka zumhning cu, Lungtak in ka ruat than tawn, Jesuh, Jesuh, mi sung Jesuh, A vel ruangah ka zumh chin.
