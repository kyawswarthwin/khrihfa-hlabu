KANNIH VAWLEI SERTU

Doh is Eb

1. Kannih vawlei sertu, Kan Pa Pathian a si, Hi hngalhnak ca kan hna a ngam, Thinphannak a um lo, Kan nih vawlei sertu, Kan Pa Pathian a si, Van le vawlei, Fing, tlang, rili, Lianhngannak in a ser.

2. Kannih vawlei sertu, Kan Pa Pathian a si, Amah lianhnak le khuaruahhar, Kannih sin a langhter, Pathian vawlei cungah, Dawhnak tampi a um, Va phun dangdang, Pangpar tam zong, Pathian lawmhnak a si.

3. Kannih vawlei sertu, Kan Pa Pathian a si, Thatlonak cu Thawng hmanhsehlaw, Uktu Pathian a si, Raldoh a dih rih lo, Jesuh nih a tei lai, Amah thawngin, vawlei le van remnak kha a si lai.
