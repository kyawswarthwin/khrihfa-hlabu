KHRIHFAMI I LAWM U

Doh is F

1. Khrihfami i lawm u, thinlung tak le aw in, Kan chimmi hi ngai hmanh u; Thawngtha! nihin ah Khrih a chuak cang Caw le laa a hmai an kun, Atu caw rawl einak kuang ah, A chuak cang Khamhtu, Nihin a chuak cang.

2. Khrihfami i lawm u, thinlung tak le aw in, Dong lo nuamhnak thawng nan theih, Lawm tuah! hi ca Khrih cu a chuak cang, Van cung innka cu a hun, Mi ca ah zungzal nuamhnak ah, Hi ca Khrih a chuak, Hi ca a chuak cang.

3. Khrihfami i lawm u, thinlung tak le aw in, Thlan zongnan tih ti lai lo, Daihnak! Khrih nih khamh awk a chuak cang, An auh nang le mi vialte, A umnak inn co awk caah, Khamhtu Khrih a chuak, Khamh awk a chuak cang.
