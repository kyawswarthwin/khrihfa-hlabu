THLACAMMI VIALTE

Doh is F

1. Lunglawmhnak thawngtha bia kha, Khua zakip ah thanh u, An thahmi Jesuh thawng in, Khamhnak thachin bia kha, A zungzal thawngthabia kha, A bia-kamnak a si; Bawi Jesuh a zummi nih, Laksawng tha an hmuh lai.

CHO: Bawipa, Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai, Bawipa Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai.

2. Khuamui chungah a um i, Sualnak lam a zulmi, Pathian dawtnak kha zoh u, Bawi Jesuh kha zum u, Lungthin nan ngeihmi vial in, A bia-kamnak zum u, Bawipa sin thlacammi nih, Rawhnak an phan lai lo.

CHO: Bawipa, Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai, Bawipa Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai.

3. Vaw leicung phorh ritmi le, Sifah a ingmi hna, Nan thilrit kha chan awkah, Jesuh nih an ngan hna, A sungbik nunnak laksawng, Mi vialte a pek hna, Zumhnak in a halmi cu khi laksawng an hmu lai.

CHO: Bawipa, Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai, Bawipa Pathian sinah thlacammi vialte nih Pathian khamhnak kha a ngai te an hmuh te lai.
