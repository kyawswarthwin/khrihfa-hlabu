NA CERHTI PUTTER

Doh is Eb

1. Pathian vel cu cerhti chuak bang, Ka lung nih hla phuah ko seh, A zaangfah tiva bang a luang, Thangthatnak hla sa u si, Hla tha bik kha kan sakter tuah, Vancung mi nih an sakmi, Na dawtnak cu tlang bang a sang, Na thangthat hla ka sa lai.

2. Bawmh duhnak lung na ka hmuhter, Na bawmhnak kha ka hmuh cang, Na vel ngeih ca'h vancung khua kha, Him tein ka phak sawk lai, Lam palhmi keimah si tikah, Jesuh nih a ka kawl ko, Thihnak in keimah tlanh awkah, A sung bik thi a thlet ko.

3. A zaangfahnak a kan pek ca, Nifate lei ka bat bang, A zaangfahnak keimah lung kha, Amah he ka lung a khat, Lam palh hmangmi ka si ngai te, Ka dawt Pathian ka hlawt tawn, Ka lung ah hmelchunhnak ka pe, Vancung ca him in chia fawn.
