BAWI CUNGNUNG BIK THANGTHAT U

Doh is G

1. Thangthat tuah u, Lei le van sertu Bawi kan Siang pa hrang, Ka thlarau thangthat tuah, na damnak le tlannak a si, A theimi nih a biakinn naih chin ko u, Hmun khat ah thangthat ti u si.

2. Thangthat tuah u, Zeizong vialte uktu Bawipa a si! A thla tangah him tein an huh phenh i an cawm ko, Na herh vialte ti le rawl zong Bawipa nih, An pek lengmang, na hngal lo maw?

3. Thangthat tuah u, Nanmah bawmtu le hupphengtu Bawi cu, A zaangfah le velngeihnak in nan mah an zohkhenh hna, Ruat than tuah u, Pathian he hawikomhnak cu, Zeitluk a that kha nan hngalh lai!.
