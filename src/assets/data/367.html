THLARAU LO CHUNGAH VORH

Doh is Ab

1. Thlarau lo chungah vorh, Nifatin vorh u, Hmunkip le ramkip ah dawtnak thlaici vorh, Thlarau thiang he vorh u, Bawipa kha zum u, Lunglawm tein tuan u laksawng kan hmu lai.

CHO: Hmai (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), panh in vorh ko u, (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), Ni a liam deng cang, (Ni a liam deng cang) Zaangzel loin tuan u, Rian aa lim hlan tiang.

2. Thlarau lo chungah vorh, Thlacam zungzal u, Kan thlaici vorhmi zong an kheu tuan ko lai, Ruah sur tertu Pathian, Zumh buin vorh u, Zing ciciai thlaikho dawh kan hmuh zau hna lai.

CHO: Hmai (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), panh in vorh ko u, (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), Ni a liam deng cang, (Ni a liam deng cang) Zaangzel loin tuan u, Rian aa lim hlan tiang.

3. Thlarau lo chungah vorh, Zumhnak tlawm hmanhseh, Mitthli tla in lungdernak um len hmanhseh, Kan rian aa lim hlan tiang, Vorh than len ko u, Zun caan a phanh tikah lawmhnak a hmun lai.

CHO: Hmai (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), panh in vorh ko u, (Hmaipanh in vorh ko u), Ni a liam deng cang, (Ni a liam deng cang) Zaangzel loin tuan u, Rian aa lim hlan tiang.
