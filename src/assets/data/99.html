KEI CA JESUH A SI

Doh is D

1. Bawipa Jesuh hlei in van ah, Bochan awk dang um lo! Ka nun chung ka sak awk hla kha, Kei ca Jesuh a si, Keimah ca sifahnak a in, Athletmi thi in a ka tawl, Pathian he a ka remter cang, Kei ca Jesuh a si.

2. Vawlei rumnak ka tlaihchan lo, Kei ca Jesuh a si! Vawlei chaw kha ka duh ti lo, Kei ca Jesuh a si, Vawlei rumnak a hmun lai lo, Zeizong vialte a rawk dih lai, Ka lunglawmhnak a dih lai lo, Kei ca Jesuh a si.

3. Pamnak sifah ka ing na in, Kei ca Jesuh a si! Ka zai lonak Bawipa a hngalh, Kei ca Jesuh a si, Ka ngeihmi le ka chaw vialte, Vawlei cung ah sung hmanhning law, Vawlei thil ka bochan lai lo, Kei ca Jesuh a si.

4. Ral kut chungah phan hmanh ninglaw, Kei ca Jesuh a si! Rawhnak nih ka kulh hmanhsehlaw, Kei ca Jesuh a si! Vawlei ral dohnak tih awk nih, Ka thlarau kha doh hmanh seh law, Bawipa min in ka tei cang lai, Kei ca Jesuh a si!
