KHUADEI VANKAM A CEU TIKAH

Doh is C

1. Khuadei vankam ceu in, Ka lung nih a au i, Jesuh thangthat siseh, Riantuan thlacam zongah, Jesuh sinah kaa hrim, Jesuh thangthat siseh.

2. Ka ngaih a chiat ahcun, Ka lung a ka hnemh tawn, Jesuh thangthat siseh, Kaa lawmhnak zor hmanhseh, Ka hnangamnak a si, Jesuh thangthat siseh.

3. Vancung zungzal lawmhnak, Aa dawh bik hla tha cu, Jesuh thangthat siseh, Khuamui tihnak zongah, A thangthat hla thawngin, Jesuh thangthat siseh.

4. Leicung i ka nun lio, Ka hla tha bikmi cu, Jesuh thangthat siseh, Amah thangthatnak hla, Zungzal in hmun ko seh, Jesuh thangthat siseh.
