KHAMHTU BAWI LUT KO SEH

Doh is Db

1. Innka hram khualpa a dir, Lut ko seh Voi tampi a ra cang i, Lut ko seh, A kal hlanah luhter tuah, Luhter tuah, A thiang Bawipa, Jesuh Khrih Pathian Fapa, Lut ko seh.

2. Na lungthin innka hung tuah, Luhter tuah, Na rauh ahcun a kal lai, Luhter tuah, Luhter tuah na hawi a si, Na thlarau kha a khamh lai, A dongh tiangin an bawmh lai, Lut ko seh.

3. A dawtnak aw na thei maw? Lut ko seh, Nihin theng ah hunpiak tuah, Lut ko seh, Innka hramah a dir ko, Lunglawmhnak kha an pek lai, A min kha na upat lai, Lut ko seh.

4. Atu vancung khualpa kha, Luhter tuah, Vancung changreu an pek lai, lut ko seh, Nangmah sualnak an thlah lai, Na hawi he naa then tikah, An lak lai vangcung inn ah, Lut ko seh.
