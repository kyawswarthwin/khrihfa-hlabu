THANGTHAT U! THANGTHAT U!

Doh is Ab

1. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh, Leicung mi nih Amah lianhnak sa u, Vanmi zong nih Jesuh lianhnak kha thangthat u, Amah dawtnak le velngeihnak chim u; Tuukhal bangin a mi kha a zohkhenh hna, A ban chungah chun nitlak a pom hna. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh! Amah lawmhnak zungzal hla in sa u!

2. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh, Kan sual ca hrengkhenh sifahnak a in, Kan fehnak zungzal kan hngatchannak kha a si, An thah cangmi Jesuh kha va bia u, Kan sifahnak a ingmi Jesuh lawm u, Amah dawtnak lawmh cawk loin a tam. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh! Amah lawmhnak zungzal hla in sa u!

3. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh, Vancung khua ah Amah thawng kha a ngan, Jesuh Khamhbawi zungzal in zungzal tiang a uk, Siangpahrang, Bawi, Profet, Tlangbawi a si, A rat tikah vawlei kha a pen dih lai, Lianhnak, Sunparnak, Bawipa ta an si. Lawm u, Lawm u, Kan dawtmi Khamhtu Bawi Jesuh! Amah lawmhnak zungzal hla in sa u!.
