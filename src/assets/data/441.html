VANCUNG INN THA, DAWTNAK RAM CU

Doh is Ab

1. Vancung inntha dawtnak ram cu, Mithiang mi hna inn a si, Annih sin kan um khawh nakhnga, Vawlei thil kha hlaw cang tuah.

CHO: Vawlei ni kha a dongh tikah, A hnu-ah inn nuam ah, A thiangmi an um nak khua ah, Van ah kan i din lai.

2. Zungzal inn nuam chung um hna cu, Inn-chungkhat bantuk an si, Inntha chung i um awkah khan, Phun zakip kha an sawm hna.

CHO: Vawlei ni kha a dongh tikah, A hnu-ah inn nuam ah, A thiangmi an um nak khua ah, Van ah kan i din lai.

3. A kan Khamhtu thihnak hmun le, Dawtnak bia kha thanter u, Hi rian-tuannak a dih hlan ah, A dawtnak bia kha zum tuah.

CHO: Vawlei ni kha a dongh tikah, A hnu-ah inn nuam ah, A thiangmi an um nak khua ah, Van ah kan i din lai.

4. Pakhat hnu pakhat in an kal, Inn-chungkhar an tling ti lo, Sihmanh-sehlaw dawtmi hna he, Ton-thannak ni a um lai.

CHO: Vawlei ni kha a dongh tikah, A hnu-ah inn nuam ah, A thiangmi an um nak khua ah, Van ah kan i din lai.
