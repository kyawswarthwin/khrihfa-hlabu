KHUAKIP AH TUANBIA CHIM AWK KAN NGEI

Doh is F

1. Khua zakip ah tuanbia chim awk kan ngei, Lam dingmi hmuhsak tu tuanbia, Biatak le zaang-fahnak tuanbia, Daihnak le ceunak tuanbia, Daihnak le ceunak tuanbia.

CHO: Muihnak a lo lai, Khua a dei lai Chun ni lin caan a rung chuak lai, Dawtnak ceunak Jesuh pennak cu vawlei ah a rungtum lai.

2. Miphun kip sinah hlasak awk kan ngei, Bawipa leiah lung thleng tu hla, Hi hla nih chiatnak a tei lai, Fei le nam a khiah dih lai, Fei le nam a khiah dih lai.

CHO: Muihnak a lo lai, Khua a dei lai Chun ni lin caan a rung chuak lai, Dawtnak ceunak Jesuh pennak cu vawlei ah a rungtum lai.

3. Khuakip ah Thawngthabia thanh awk kan ngei, Van in mi uktu Pathian nih, A Fa Khamhtu a run thlah i, Pathan dawtnak a langhter, Pathian dawtnak a langhter.

CHO: Muihnak a lo lai, Khua a dei lai Chun ni lin caan a rung chuak lai, Dawtnak ceunak Jesuh pennak cu vawlei ah a rungtum lai.

4. Khuakip ah Khamhbawi hmuhsak awk kan ngei, Ngaih-chiatnak lam a zulmi kha; Amah thawngin miphun kip nih, Pathian dinnak an hngalh lai, Pathian dinnak an hngalh lai.

CHO: Muihnak a lo lai, Khua a dei lai Chun ni lin caan a rung chuak lai, Dawtnak ceunak Jesuh pennak cu vawlei ah a rungtum lai.
