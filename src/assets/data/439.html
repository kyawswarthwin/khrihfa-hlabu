KHRIHFA, MANG THA

Doh is Ab

1. Ka dawt te it law dai tein i din, Na Khamhtu tang cungah na lu chia tuah, Kannih nakin Jesuh nih an dawt deuh, Mangtha! Mangtha! Mangtha!

2. Ngakchia te bang dai tein it ko tuah, Ngaih-chiatnak nih an hlau kho ti lai lo, Zungzal dong lo dinhnak tha na phan cang, Mangtha! Mangtha! Mangtha!

3. Vawlei in muihnak a loh dih hlan tiang, Caan dongh tikah a kan khawmh dih hlan tiang, Khua ceu maw vialte an loh dih hlan tiang, Mangtha! Mangtha! Mangtha!

4. Dawtnak thiang nih dawh in an ser hlan tiang, Pathian na lawhnak ni bang a tlang lai, Sui luchin cu nang caah a put lai, Mangtha! Mangtha! Mangtha!

5. A thutdan hmaiah kan i tong than lai, A thil pekmi zong kan i thuam te lai, A kan hngalh bang kannih kan hngalh hlan tiang, Mangtha! Mangtha! Mangtha!
