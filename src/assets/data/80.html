RA TUAH, RA TUAH, EMMANUEL

Doh is E

1. Ra tuah, ra tuah, Emmanuel, Israel thongtla run tlan hna, Ngaihchiat lunghno an tah lio ah, Pathian Fapa a rat hlan tiang.

CHO: Lawm tuah! Lawm tuah! Emmanuel, A ra than lai, Maw Israel.

2. Ra tuah, ra tuah, Nang Je-si-nge, Satan kut in na mi luatter, Hell chungin na mi khamh tuah hna, Thihnak ralteinak run pe hna.

CHO: Lawm tuah! Lawm tuah! Emmanuel, A ra than lai, Maw Israel.

3. Ra tuah, Ceunak, Bawi kan lungthin, Na rat thawngin lawmhter ko tuah, Muihnak kha run ceuter ko tuah, Khuadawm tianter law ceu kan pe.

CHO: Lawm tuah! Lawm tuah! Emmanuel, A ra than lai, Maw Israel.

4. Ra tuah David Fa, Kan Bawipa, Vancung innka kan hunpiak tuah, Vancung ram ah kan huhphenh tuah, Lam har kha kan khampiak ko sawh, Lawm tuah! Lawm tuah! Emmanuel a ra than lai, Maw Israel.

CHO: Lawm tuah! Lawm tuah! Emmanuel, A ra than lai, Maw Israel.
