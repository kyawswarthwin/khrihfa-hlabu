HLA THAR AN SA

Doh is C

1. Vancung khua chungah kan hawi le hla an sa, A kan Khamhbawi a min kha an thangthat cio, Vawleicung sifah retheihnak an philh cang, Mithiang mi nih lunglawmhnak hla thar an sa.
