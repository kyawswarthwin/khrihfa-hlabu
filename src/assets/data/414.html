THAWNGTHA BIA DARKHING TUM TUAH

Doh is F

1. Lung-lawmhnak thawngtha bia kha Pengkhat hnu pengkhat thanh u, Darkhing thawng bang khua zakip ah, Khamhbawi bia kha thanter u; Pathian a Fapa kha, A zummi a dihlak nih, Rawhnak ah an phan bal lai lo, Zungzal nunnak an hmuh lai.

CHO: Lawmhnak bia thanter tuah, Pengkhat hnu pengkhat thanh u, Lawmhnak via man loin, Nangmah le kei ca a si.

2. Mi vialte hna ra tuah u, Darkhing thawng in an auh hna, Pathian dawtnak a ngan caah, Hlaw hin hlah u, u nau hna, Nunnak changreu a si, Hi changreu a eimi cu, An sualnak kha tam hmanh sehlaw, Hawhra bang a ranter lai.

CHO: Lawmhnak bia thanter tuah, Pengkhat hnu pengkhat thanh u, Lawmhnak via man loin, Nangmah le kei ca a si.

3. Pathian bia a ngai lomi, Sifahnak kha an in lai, Khamhbawi aunak aw thawng a nem, Ralrinnak a kan pek tawn, Khamhnak hmuh awk caah, Rau loin chuak cio tuah u, Nan hnulei kha zoh ti hlah u, Nan zoh ahcun nan liam lai.

CHO: Lawmhnak bia thanter tuah, Pengkhat hnu pengkhat thanh u, Lawmhnak via man loin, Nangmah le kei ca a si.
