KEIMAH PHILHLONAK AH

Doh is D

1. Kan in biak maw Bawipa, Khuaruahhar na vel ca, Nihin tiangin na kan huhphenh ko, Na thatnak kha kan lawmh, dong lo na dawtnak, Nangmah hmaiah kun in, Hmaizahnak kan in pek.

CHO: Nangmah na nawlbia sung bangin, Nihin ah hin, Na cabuai ah nang he kan i chawn, "Mah hi nan tuah lai, Keimah philhlonak ah" Na rak timi kha kan ruat than.

2. Na nunnak changreu kha, Ei awkah kan tlak lo, A til-tlawt khawmh awk zong kan tlak lo, Siseh nangmah thatnak, thawngin kan ra i, Na velngeihnak thawng in, Kannih kan i tel ve.

CHO: Nangmah na nawlbia sung bangin, Nihin ah hin, Na cabuai ah nang he kan i chawn, "Mah hi nan tuah lai, Keimah philhlonak ah" Na rak timi kha kan ruat than.

3. Kannih nih thazang thar, Ruahnak thar la u si, Zumhnak in kan hmuhmi thil thawng hin, Bawipa a rat than tiang, amah thihnak kha, Vancung khua kan phak tiangin, Langhter cio u si.

CHO: Nangmah na nawlbia sung bangin, Nihin ah hin, Na cabuai ah nang he kan i chawn, "Mah hi nan tuah lai, Keimah philhlonak ah" Na rak timi kha kan ruat than.
