RAWL TUANTU A PUMH HNA

Doh is Bb

1. Sunparnak ram in hlasak na thei maw? Lawmhnak hlasak na thei maw? Lungtho tein kan faphal pum u si, A donghnak caan a phan deng cang,

CHO: Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Aan dawh hmaipanh cu kan hmuh cang lai, Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Rawltuan tu a pumh hna lai.

2. Kan dawtmi hna nih, sunparnak ram in, Lunglawm in an run kan zoh, An rian an lim i harnak an lonh cang, Kamhmi sui luchin an hmuh cang,

CHO: Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Aan dawh hmaipanh cu kan hmuh cang lai, Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Rawltuan tu a pumh hna lai.

3. Sunparnak ram in hladawh a rung thang, Kan lung thin cu a thawnter, Cu hla aw dawh nih nunnak a thanter, Ni lin tang rian kan tuan lioah,

CHO: Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Aan dawh hmaipanh cu kan hmuh cang lai, Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Rawltuan tu a pumh hna lai.

4. Sunparnak ram in hla dawh a rung thang; Lawmhnak le par dawh enhnak, A nungmi zumhnak thawngin kan hngalh cang, Kannih zong kan sa ti hna lai.

CHO: Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Aan dawh hmaipanh cu kan hmuh cang lai, Rawltuan uktu Bawipa a ra deng cang, Rawltuan tu a pumh hna lai.
