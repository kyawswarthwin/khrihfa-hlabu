KA LAM JESUH A KA HMUHSAK

Doh is Ab

1. Ka lam Jesuh a ka hmuhsak, Zei bia dah ka herh rih hnga? Ka lu nun lioah domh-tlaitu, Zumh sualnak a um hnga maw? Vancung daihnak le hna-ngamnak, Jesuh zumhnak in ka hmuh, Vawlei i ka huahmi vialte, Jesuh ding tein a ceih. Vawlei i ka hmuhmi vialte, Jesuh ding tein a ceih.

2. Lamhar ah ka tha a nuamter, Lam vialte a ka hmuhsak, Hnek-saknak ka inn khawh nakhnga, Nunnak changreu a ka pek, Ka kal lio i ka bat tikah, Ka thlarau tihal hmanh seh, Ka hmailei ah lungpi chungin, Lawmhnak cerhti luan ka hmuh, Ka hmailei ah lungpi chungin, Lawmhnak cerhti luan ka hmuh,

3. Ka lam Jesuh a ka hmuhsak, Zeitluk in dah a ka dawt, Vancung ka Pa inn daihnak ah, Ka um nak-hnga bia a kam, Ka thlarau kha hla a sa i, Vancung ah a zuan tikah, Lam kha Jesuh a ka hmuhsak, Zungzal hla in ka sak lai; Lam kha Jesuh a ka hmuhsak, Zungzal hla in ka sak lai.
