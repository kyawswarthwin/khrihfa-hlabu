LAA FATE CIT IN

Doh is F

1. Bawipa le zultu hna cu Beth-fath khua an phak tikah, Zultu pahnih cu hmaiah a thlah hna, Cit bal lo laa fate cu nan rak hruai lai, a ti hna, Profet nih rak chimhchung mi bia tlinnak ah a si.

CHO: Zion fanu cu va chim u, "Na siangpahrang cu na sinah, Nunnem toidor in laa fate cit in a ra." Laa fate cit in kan Bawipa Je-ru-sa-lem khuapi lei ah "Ho-sa-na" mi run au thawng he a va lut.

2. Khua-li-pi an luh lioah ngakchuan semmawng tiang an au, Laa fate le Bawipa puanpar an kai, Tum-hnah put in an thangthat "Ho-sa-na" atu kan khamh, Mikhamhtu Bawi a sinak cu fiang tein a lang.

CHO: Zion fanu cu va chim u, "Na siangpahrang cu na sinah, Nunnem toidor in laa fate cit in a ra." Laa fate cit in kan Bawipa Je-ru-sa-lem khuapi lei ah "Ho-sa-na" mi run au thawng he a va lut.

3. A rauh hlanah kan Bawipa an rawinak caan a hung phan, Vailam ah khenh ko u tiah, an au than, Nihin ah kan Bawi a si, Thaizing ah kan hlawt than tawn, Kan sining cu kanmah te nih ruat than cio u sih.

CHO: Zion fanu cu va chim u, "Na siangpahrang cu na sinah, Nunnem toidor in laa fate cit in a ra." Laa fate cit in kan Bawipa Je-ru-sa-lem khuapi lei ah "Ho-sa-na" mi run au thawng he a va lut.
