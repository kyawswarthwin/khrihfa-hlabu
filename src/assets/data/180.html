KHRIHFA RALKAP KAL U

Doh is F

1. Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Dohnak ah kal u, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u; Jesuh kan Siangpahrang, Hmaiah a kan sak, Raldoh zuam a kalmi, Jesuh kha zoh u,

CHO: Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Ral dohnak ah kal, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u.

2. Jesuh min an theih ah, Rulhreu mi an tli, Khrihfa ralkapmi nih, Teinak hmu u si, Aunak thawng an theih in, Rulhreu pennak rawk, U le nau au len u, Lawmhnak in thangthat.

CHO: Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Ral dohnak ah kal, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u.

3. Ralkap rian an tuan bang, Khrihfabu an tuah, Hmailei mithiang keneh, Kannih kan zulh hna, Kannih then lo in kawp, Pum khat te kan si, Dawtnak, zumhnak, bochannak, Kan lungtak a si.

CHO: Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Ral dohnak ah kal, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u.

4. Vawlei mi-lian pennak, An rawk len nain, Bawi Jesuh pennak kha, Zungzal a hmun ko, Rulhreu puarpennak kha, Teinak an hmu lo, Jesuh bia a kan kam, Amah bia a hmun.

CHO: Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Ral dohnak ah kal, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u.

5. Vawlei mi vialte hna, Lut Khrihfabu ah, Nan teinak hlasaknak, Nannih zong sa u; Rel cawk lo chan lakah, Van le vawlei mi, Thangthat le upatnak, Jesuh pe u si.

CHO: Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Ral dohnak ah kal, Hmailei ah a kalmi Jesuh kha zoh u.
