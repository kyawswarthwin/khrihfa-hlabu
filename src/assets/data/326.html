HMAI TONH IN

Doh is Bb

1. Hmaitonh in ka khamhtu Khrih cu, Ka hoi kho ve te hnga maw, A ka lak tikah ka hoi lai, Kei ca a thimi Khrih cu.

CHO: Hmaitonh in ka hoi khawh te lai, Arfi pinlei van ah khin, Hmaitonh in a suparnak he, Ka hmuh te lai tu hnuah.

2. Zumhnak lawngin atu ka hmuh, Muihnak nih phen hmanhseh law, Siseh Ni sung bik a ra lio, A sunpar lianhngannak he.

CHO: Hmaitonh in ka hoi khawh te lai, Arfi pinlei van ah khin, Hmaitonh in a suparnak he, Ka hmuh te lai tu hnuah.

3. Zeitluk thanuamnak dah si lai, Ngaihchiat sifah loh tik ah, Lam kawi vialte samh an si lai, Muihnak zong a um lai lo.

CHO: Hmaitonh in ka hoi khawh te lai, Arfi pinlei van ah khin, Hmaitonh in a suparnak he, Ka hmuh te lai tu hnuah.

4. Hmaitonh in tonnak caan nuam bik, Hmaitonh in hmuh le hngalh caan, A ka dawtu Jesuh Khrih he, Hmaitonh in ton caan a si.

CHO: Hmaitonh in ka hoi khawh te lai, Arfi pinlei van ah khin, Hmaitonh in a suparnak he, Ka hmuh te lai tu hnuah.
