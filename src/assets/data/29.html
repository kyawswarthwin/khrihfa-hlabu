KA HNGAK THIUTHEO

Doh is G

1. Ka hngah thiutheo, Ni a hun i mer i chunhnu a phanhtiang, Ka hngah thiutheo, Zanlei khuamawt, Van kam ceu a loh dih tiang, Chun laicer in zantim khuamawt caan ah a hun i thlen hnu, Fino rual fikceu a hung keh-Maw chilchial vankam ceu tiang.

2. Ka hngah thiutheo, Rawl tuantu hna fa-phal put in an rat tiang, Vaupi nilin an hung liam hnu, Chimthli nih a hun zulh bang, Rawl tuantu hna i hnawh ko u, ka lungthin theipar zun u, Nunnak par dawh cu uai ser in liam tuan ko ning ti ka duh.

3. Ka hngah thiutheo, Lungvar kutka van-mi run nih an hun tiang, Sifak rethei si hmanh ninglaw, Cu kutka cu kaa ruahchan, Lam hlapi i an hlasak thawng, an kal thawng cu ka theih hna, An ka auh lai caan lawng ka hngak, An aan ngaih in hngak ko ning.

4. A ceu bikmi inn dawh ka hngak, Mithmuh bal lo inn dawh bik, Par tha en lio nunnak thingkung, Fing le tlang nih hring dildel, Ka khamhnak caan tlin lai ka hngak, Ka Khamhtu pek laimi kha, Sualnak vialte an lo dih lai, Ngaihchiat lungkuai an lo lai.
