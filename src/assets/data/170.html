JESUH A RA LIO

Doh is Bb

1. Hlaphuah u si, Pathian Fa a tum lai, Thi in khamhnak a hmumi vialte ca, A rung tum lai i a kan cawi dih lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai.

CHO: Jesuh a rungtum lai, rung tum than lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai; (a rung tum than lai) Khua kip ah Pathian lawmhnak hla thek u, Jesu a rung tum than lai.

2. Jesuh tum tikah mithi an tho lai, Kan sianlomi he kan i tong dih lai, Vancung Pathian sinah kan kai dih lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai.

CHO: Jesuh a rungtum lai, rung tum than lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai; (a rung tum than lai) Khua kip ah Pathian lawmhnak hla thek u, Jesu a rung tum than lai.

3. Jesuh a tum lai, mithiang a khamh lai, Ral thawhnak vawlei kha a daihter lai, Ngaihchiat thinphan sualnak a lo dih lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai.

CHO: Jesuh a rungtum lai, rung tum than lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai; (a rung tum than lai) Khua kip ah Pathian lawmhnak hla thek u, Jesu a rung tum than lai.

4. Ka ra lai tiah biakam a pel lai lo, Pathian thim ciami ahodah an si? Jesuh a ngangmi ahodah an si? Jesuh a rung tum than lai.

CHO: Jesuh a rungtum lai, rung tum than lai, Jesuh a rung tum than lai; (a rung tum than lai) Khua kip ah Pathian lawmhnak hla thek u, Jesu a rung tum than lai.
