KHAMHNAK

Doh is C

1. Bawi Jesuh Khrih zaangfahnak kha, Lawm in sa cio tuah u; Vawlei vialte a zelh lai ca, Nan lunglawm cio tuah u!

CHO: A kan khamhnak thawngtha kha, Atu a tlung cang, A kan Khambawi Jesuh nih, Atu an khamh cang.

2. Mitcaw mi nih Jesuh zoh u, Ngaihchiami lawm tuah u; Jesuh nih an chuahter cang hna, Thong chung ummi lawm tuah.

CHO: A kan khamhnak thawngtha kha, Atu a tlung cang, A kan Khambawi Jesuh nih, Atu an khamh cang.

3. A kan dawt ca Jesuh a thi, thangthat a tlak ngaingai; Vawlei cungah daihnak um seh, Pathian thangtha ko seh.

CHO: A kan khamhnak thawngtha kha, Atu a tlung cang, A kan Khambawi Jesuh nih, Atu an khamh cang.
