KEIMAH KHAMHTU

Doh is Ab

1. Khamhbawi dawtnak a ngan ngaingai, Lunglawmhnak hla ka sa lai, Sualnak in keimah tlanh awkah, Vai-lamtahnak ah thi cang.

CHO: Bawipa thangthatnak hlaka sa, A thi in a ka tawl cang; Vailamtah nak kha a in i, Ka sualnak kha a thlah cang.

2. Vancung ro kha co awk ca-ah, A tha bik bia a kan chimh, A kan dawtnak a ngan ca-ah, Kan sualnak kha a thlah cang.

CHO: Bawipa thangthatnak hlaka sa, A thi in a ka tawl cang; Vailamtah nak kha a in i, Ka sualnak kha a thlah cang.

3. A kan Khamhbawi a min a thang, Nifate ka thangthat lai, Ka thih tikah hell chung biaceih, Phakter kha a ka duh lo.

CHO: Bawipa thangthatnak hlaka sa, A thi in a ka tawl cang; Vailamtah nak kha a in i, Ka sualnak kha a thlah cang.

4. A kan Khamhbawi dawtnak a ngan, Hla tha in ka thangthat lai, Zungzal nunnak ram chungah khin, Amah he kan um lai ta.

CHO: Bawipa thangthatnak hlaka sa, A thi in a ka tawl cang; Vailamtah nak kha a in i, Ka sualnak kha a thlah cang.
