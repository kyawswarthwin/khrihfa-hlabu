SUNPAR LUCHIN CHINH U

Doh is F

1. Vawlei mi vialte nih zoh tuah, Ngaihchiatnak a ing Jesuh, A ral vialte a tei dih cang, Mi vialte nih khan an biak.

CHO: Sun-par luchin sun-par luchin, Bawi Jesuh kha chinhter tuah. A lianngan bik Bawi Jesuh kha Siangpahrang, siangpahrang chin.

2. Vancung khua ummi vialte nih, Bawi Jesuh a thang an that, Biaceih thutdan cung an thutter, Vancung mi nih an lawmh tawn.

CHO: Sun-par luchin sun-par luchin, Bawi Jesuh kha chinhter tuah. A lianngan bik Bawi Jesuh kha Siangpahrang, siangpahrang chin.

3. Hling luchin kha misual mi nih, Bawi Jesuh kha an chinhter, Mithiang mi le vancung mi nih, Jesuh min kha an thangthat.

CHO: Sun-par luchin sun-par luchin, Bawi Jesuh kha chinhter tuah. A lianngan bik Bawi Jesuh kha Siangpahrang, siangpahrang chin.

4. Amah hlorh in au cio tuah u! Ral tei lawmhnak kha an tuah. Sunparnak ngan bik a hmuh cang. Zohtuah! Bawi Jesuh a si.

CHO: Sun-par luchin sun-par luchin, Bawi Jesuh kha chinhter tuah. A lianngan bik Bawi Jesuh kha Siangpahrang, siangpahrang chin.
