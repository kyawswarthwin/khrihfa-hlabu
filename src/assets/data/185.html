KA ZUMHMI KA HNGALH

Doh is Eb

1. Pathian nih zei ca velngeihnak a ka pek ka hngal lo, Dawtnak in zeica tlanh tlak lo, A ka tlanh ka hngal lo.

CHO: Sihmansehlaw, ka zumhmi ka hngalh, Ka Pathian sinah ka chiahmi vialte, Khi ni tiang a huhphenh khawh lai, Cu-caah ka ningzak lo.

2. Zeitin khamh hmuhnak zumhnak hi a ka pek ka hngal lo, Ka lungchung ah A-mah bia nih, Daihnak a ka pek zong.

CHO: Sihmansehlaw, ka zumhmi ka hngalh, Ka Pathian sinah ka chiahmi vialte, Khi ni tiang a huhphenh khawh lai, Cu-caah ka ningzak lo.

3. Zeitin Thlarau nih misual lung a thlen hi ka hngal lo, Baibal in Jesuh kong a thanh, Zumhnak lung a kan pek.

CHO: Sihmansehlaw, ka zumhmi ka hngalh, Ka Pathian sinah ka chiahmi vialte, Khi ni tiang a huhphenh khawh lai, Cu-caah ka ningzak lo.

4. Zeitik ka Bawi rat lai hngalh lo, Zan ah maw chun ceu ah, Nelrawn ah maw kan kal ti lai, Van ah dah kan ton lai?.

CHO: Sihmansehlaw, ka zumhmi ka hngalh, Ka Pathian sinah ka chiahmi vialte, Khi ni tiang a huhphenh khawh lai, Cu-caah ka ningzak lo.
