NIHIN RUNG TUM SEHLAW

Doh is D

1. Jesuh vawlei ah a tum than lai, Nihin tum sual seh law, Thawnnak le dawtnak in pen awkah, Nihin rung tum sehlaw, A thimmi ngaknu lak awkah, A tlanhmi le tawlmi vialte, Vawlei khuazakip ah an um, Nihin rungtum sehlaw.

CHO: Thangthat ko ning, ka lung a lawmhter lai, Thangthat ko ning, Luchin ka chinh tikah, Thangthat ko ning, A lam remh awk caah, Thangthat ko ning, Jesuh a tum te lai.

2. Satan uknak a ciam zau te lai, Nihin va ciam seh law! Ngaihchiat tahhram a um ti lai lo, Nihin ah siseh law, Khrih ah a thimi an tho lai, Khrih ton awk van in an ngan lai, Khi sunpar zeitik kan hmuh lai, Nihin ah sisehlaw.

CHO: Thangthat ko ning, ka lung a lawmhter lai, Thangthat ko ning, Luchin ka chinh tikah, Thangthat ko ning, A lam remh awk caah, Thangthat ko ning, Jesuh a tum te lai.

3. Zumhfek le miding kan si lai maw? Nihin a tum ahcun, Tih loin, lunglawm in hngak u si, Nihin a rat ahcun, A rat lai hmelchunh a karh chin, Nichuahlei in ceu a lang cang, Hngak ko, a caan a nai deng cang, Nihin tum sual sehlaw.

CHO: Thangthat ko ning, ka lung a lawmhter lai, Thangthat ko ning, Luchin ka chinh tikah, Thangthat ko ning, A lam remh awk caah, Thangthat ko ning, Jesuh a tum te lai.
