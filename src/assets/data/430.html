BEAULAH RAM AH KAN UM

Doh is Ab

1. Lam hlatpi hna-hnawhnak thawng kha ka hna nih a theih i, Vawlei sualnak nih a ka kulh dih ti kha ka hngalh, Lung awtawm le tihnak nih khan ka lem hmanh hna seh law, An ka thial kho lai lo, Beaulah ram in.

CHO: Beau-lah ram chungah ka um, Ram nuambik ram tha chung ah, (chungah) Khi ram ah, tiput le cerh dong loin an luang ko, Dong loin manna changreu a um, A zungzal in ka khim ko, Beaulah ram chungah ka um caah.

2. Vawlei cung ah hin zumh lonak tilet nganpi a tho, Mi nih kan ral Satan kha doh awk a herh zungzal, Sihmanhseh Pathian bia ralhruang chungah ka um ca-ah, A ka phan kho lai lo, Beaulah ram ah.

3. Thlichia kha fakpi in hrang hmanhsehlaw ka tih lai lo, Pathian kut nih a ka huhphenh ca-ah ka him ko, Beaulah ram chungah cun tihphan awk zeihmanh a um lo, Zungzal in ka him ko, Beaulah ram ah.

4. Pathian riantuannak ka ruah tikah ka khuaruah a har, A bia kha ka theih tikah ka lung a lawm ngaingai, Keimah khamhnak ca i a riantuannak ka hmuh tikah, Lunglawm in ka um lai, Beaulah ram ah.
