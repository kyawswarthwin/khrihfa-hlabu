AA DAWH BIK JESUH

Doh is F

1. Aa dawh bik Jesuh, Zeizong vialte pengtu, Pathian le minung i Fapa, Nangmah kan bochan, Nangmah kan upat, Nang ka thlarau sunpar luchin.

2. Ram-hring an i dawh, Thing an i dawh chinchin, Sunparnak in an i thuam dih, Jesuh aa dawh deuh, Jesuh a thiang deuh, Ngaihchiami lung a lawmhter hna.

3. Nitlang aa dawh ko, Thlapa aa dawh chinchin, Vancung arfi vialte zong khi, Vanmi vialte nih, An thangthat leng in, Jesuh cu a thiang i a ceu.
