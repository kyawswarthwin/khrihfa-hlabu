AA HUNG CIAMI HAUKA

Doh is C

1. Aa hung ciami hauka chungin, Vailam-tah ceunak a ra, Jesuh dawtnak ni ceu bangin, Kannih sinah a lang cang.

CHO: Jesuh dawtnak a tam bik ca, Khi hauka cu keimah luhnak, Keimah caah, Kei ca an hun cia cang.

2. Khamhnak kha a duhmi vialte, Hi hauka mi dihlak ca, Chaw ngei, ngei lo, a lian, a hme, Mi phunkip an zapi ca.

CHO: Jesuh dawtnak a tam bik ca, Khi hauka cu keimah luhnak, Keimah caah, Kei ca an hun cia cang.

3. Thinphang le aw-lok-chongmi ca, Zaangfahnak kaa a hun ko, Vailam-tahnak nan put caah, Nunnak laksawng nan hmuh lai.

CHO: Jesuh dawtnak a tam bik ca, Khi hauka cu keimah luhnak, Keimah caah, Kei ca an hun cia cang.

4. Mah hin kan put vailamtahnak, Thihnak ral ah kan chiah lai, Nunnak laksawng kan i lak i, Jesuh kha kan dawt chin lai.

CHO: Jesuh dawtnak a tam bik ca, Khi hauka cu keimah luhnak, Keimah caah, Kei ca an hun cia cang.
