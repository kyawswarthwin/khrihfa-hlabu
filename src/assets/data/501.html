KILVENG ZUNGZAL HNA

Doh is D

1. Cung chin kan biak Pathian samhri sihnak thawngin thih hlan leng ti dingin fonh nan si bangin, Khrihfa inn-chungkhar tha, Kom hawi phai len awkin, Thluachuah cung vanni za hmu ko u.

CHO: Atu thit-umnak hi chan chung nguh in fek seh, Maw Bawipa na dawtnak in run tem hna! An i dawtnak thuk in hram thla kho ve dingah, Na Thiang thlarau in kilveng zungzal hna.

2. Nunnak hawikom sung bik hngatchan tlak si u law hrin nu sorpa thlua chuah in pe ko hna seh, Nunnak lam har chungah zumhtlak in i daw u, Bawipa dawtnak muihmel langhter u.

CHO: Atu thit-umnak hi chan chung nguh in fek seh, Maw Bawipa na dawtnak in run tem hna! An i dawtnak thuk in hram thla kho ve dingah, Na Thiang thlarau in kilveng zungzal hna.

3. Hmaizah le zawnruahnak Khrihfa nuncan sung bik nannih innchungkhar ah leng ve zungzal seh, Leng le val zohchunh awk hmailei lam-sialtu ah, Bawipa nih in daw zungzal hna seh.

CHO: Atu thit-umnak hi chan chung nguh in fek seh, Maw Bawipa na dawtnak in run tem hna! An i dawtnak thuk in hram thla kho ve dingah, Na Thiang thlarau in kilveng zungzal hna.
