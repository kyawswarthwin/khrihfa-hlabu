A TANG CHUNGAH KA UM!

Doh is A

1. Lawmhnak le hawikomhnak tha ka hmuh cang, A zungzal, A tang chungah ka um, Lunglawmhnak le daihnak ah ka um cang, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.

CHO: Amah hngatchan, Tihphannak in ka luat dih cang, Amah hngat chan, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.

2. Lam ka kal lioah phannak a um lo, A zungzal, A tang chungah ka um, Nifate in lam a ceu chin lengmang, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.

CHO: Amah hngatchan, Tihphannak in ka luat dih cang, Amah hngat chan, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.

3. Phannak le tihnak a um kho hnga maw, A zungzal, A tang chungah ka um, Bawipa naih tikah, Lawmhnak kha ka hmuh, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.

CHO: Amah hngatchan, Tihphannak in ka luat dih cang, Amah hngat chan, A zungzal a tang chungah ka um.
