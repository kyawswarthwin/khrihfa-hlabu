KA LUNG CHUNGAH AW THLUM A RUNG THANG

Doh is Ab

1. Bawi Jesuh nih hla dawh a ka pek, Van in a rungmi a si, Khi nak dawh deuh hla aw a um bal lo, Dawtnak hla aw thlum a si.

CHO: Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Van aw thawng he aa remmi hla dawh cu a thang, Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Dawtnak hla aw thlum a rung thang.

2. Kal-va-ry a thimi Khrih ka dawt, Ka sual a tawl dih caah, Ka lung chungah hla aw a khumh tak cang, Ka lung chung a um ka hngalh.

CHO: Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Van aw thawng he aa remmi hla dawh cu a thang, Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Dawtnak hla aw thlum a rung thang.

3. Sunparnak hla dong lo a si lai, Vancungmi he kan sak lai, Ngaih nuammi thangthatnak aw asi lai, Van darkhing an tum tikah.

CHO: Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Van aw thawng he aa remmi hla dawh cu a thang, Ka lung chungah aw thlum a rung thang, Dawtnak hla aw thlum a rung thang.
