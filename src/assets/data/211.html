THLICHIA A HRANGMI IN

Doh is C

1. Totho thlichia hrangmi chung le, Ngaihchiat tilet a thomi in, Daihnak le dornak hmun a um, Velngeihnak thutdan toi ah khan.

2. Jesuh nih a thisen sungin, Kannih vialte a kan tlanh cang, Thisen cawkmi velngeih thutdan, Kan ca hnangamnak hmun a si.

3. Thlarau lung khat tlonlen tinak, Hawikom rualremnak hmun a um, Dang tlei hmanh zumh in an leng ti, Velngeihnak thutdan velchum ah.

4. Va bangin van ah kan zuang lai, Sual hnahnawh ti lo hmun ah khin; Pathian sunpar lianhngannak nih, Velngeih thutdan a khuh tikah.
