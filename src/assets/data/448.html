AA DAWH INNKA AN HUN TIKAH

Doh is G

1. Sual-muihnak chungah hin sau kan vak lai lo, Zungzal khuatha nifa kan naih, Ceunak Pathian khua dawh chung kan luh tikah, Aa dawh innka khi an hun lai.

CHO: Aa dawh inn-ka khi an hun ah (hun lai) Aa dawh inn-ka khi an hun lai (hun lai) Kan Bawi kan hmuh tikah kan lawmh a tling lai, Aa dawh inn-ka an hun tikah.

2. Dinhnak a tha khua khi phak ka duh ko cang, Innka hun caan a tlun tikah, Hna-hnawhnak le vawlei sualnak a lo lai, Aa dawh inn-ka an hun tikah.

3. Zawtfah, ngaihchiat tahnak zong a lo dih lai, Aa dawh kutka an hun tikah, Thihnak thenmi vialte kan ton dih hna lai, Aa dawh innka an hun tikah.

4. Aa dawhmi sunparnak in kan i thuam lai, A dawh kutka an hun tikah, A tha hla kha ralchan ah kan sa ti lai, Tuufa thah cia kan thangthat lai.
