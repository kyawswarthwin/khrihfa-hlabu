PATHIAN CU THANGTHAT SI KO SEH

Doh is Ab.

1. Zeizong sertu Pathian, thangthat si ko seh, Vawlei a dawt ca'h a Fa-pa a kan pek; Sual ngaihthiam-nak ca a nunnak kha a sian, Mi kip ca'h a nunnak kutka a hun cang.

CHO: Bawipa cu, thangthat u, Khua zakip cul dih seh, Bawipa cu, thangthat u, Mi kip i lawm hna seh, Fapa Jesuh thawngin Pathian cu panh u. Zeizong vialte sertu Pathian thangthat u.

2. Maw Khamhnak tling thisen in a kan tlanhnak, A zummi kip sin i Pathian biakamnak; Lungtak in a zummi misual bik hmanh nih, Jesuh sinin sual ngaihthiamnak a hmuh colh.

CHO: Bawipa cu, thangthat u, Khua zakip cul dih seh, Bawipa cu, thangthat u, Mi kip i lawm hna seh, Fapa Jesuh thawngin Pathian cu panh u. Zeizong vialte sertu Pathian thangthat u.

3. A sermi thil vialte kong a kan cawnpiak, Fapa Jesuh thawngin lawmhnak a sang chin; Sihmanhseh Jesuh kan hmuh te tikah cun, Khuaruahhar in lawmhnak a sang chinchin lai.
