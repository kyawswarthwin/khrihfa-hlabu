MAW PATHIAN, NANG VELNGEI BIK

Doh is D

1. Maw Pathian, nang velngei bik, Dawtnak a tling bikmi, Na thlua-chuahnak a sung bik, An cungah tlunter law, Na lianh-ngannak ruat tiin, Na thluachuah hmuhter hna, Thih tiang in um ti awkah, An bia-kamnak fehter.

2. Vawlei na um lioah khan, Kana khua na phan i, Thit-umnak na lawmhpi hna, Misur hang na karhter, A tu an i thitnak hi, Lawmhpi law thluachuah pe, An i duhnak karhter law, Na min upat hna seh.

3. Nang an in bochan nakhnga, Maw Pathian bawm hna law, Ngaihchiat harnak an in ah, Nang nih umpi ve hna, An nunnak ni nih liam in, Lei kaltak hna hmanhseh, Zungzal um ti awk caah, Na inn ah hruai ve hna.
