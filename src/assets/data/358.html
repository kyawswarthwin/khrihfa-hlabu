LAWMHNAK THLAM KA LUNG CHUNGAH

Doh is Ab

1. Lawmhnak thlam ka lung chungah a um, Jesuh ceunak caah, Hi ceunak ni nak in ceu deuh, Ka lung chung ah a um.

CHO: Maw hi lawmhnak, lawmhnak sungbik, Keimah lung chungah lawmhnak a um, Jesuh ceunak a langh tikah, Lawmhnak ka lung chung a khat.

2. Hla dawh thlam ka lungchungah a um, Ka Bawipa thangthat nak; Holh in ka chim khawh lomi hla, Jesuh nih a theih ko.

CHO: Maw hi lawmhnak, lawmhnak sungbik, Keimah lung chungah lawmhnak a um, Jesuh ceunak a langh tikah, Lawmhnak ka lung chung a khat.

3. Thing ram hnah a corh than caan bangin, Ka lung corhnak a um, Va kai lawmhnak caan a tlun bang, Ka lung-lawmhnak a tlung.

CHO: Maw hi lawmhnak, lawmhnak sungbik, Keimah lung chungah lawmhnak a um, Jesuh ceunak a langh tikah, Lawmhnak ka lung chung a khat.

4. Thangthat, ruah-channak, lawmhnak, dawtnak, Ka lung ah a um i, Atu le hmailei thluachuah ca, Lunglawmhnak kha ka ngei.

CHO: Maw hi lawmhnak, lawmhnak sungbik, Keimah lung chungah lawmhnak a um, Jesuh ceunak a langh tikah, Lawmhnak ka lung chung a khat.
