NA NGAIHCHIATNAK HLAW TUAH

Doh is Bb

1. Hi vawlei ngaih-chiatnak vialte kha hlaw tuah, Zanlei caan tikah khan, Duhsah kan ruah lai Khuamui chungah chim tuah, Biatak in vaa pe, Jesuh sinah hal tuah, Thachin an pek lai.

2. Jesuh sinah chim tuah, Ngaih-chiatnak a hngalh, Jesuh na chimh ahcun, Thachin an pek lai. Na lam kha a ceu cang, Va kal law, khawm tuah, Hal tuah, na ngaih-chiatnak, Na thil a zaang lai.

3. Ngaihchiat lungrawhnak he, Khuamui hmanhsehlaw, Nangmah nak sifakmi, Va bawm zungzal hna. Ngaih-chiatnak kha hlaw tuah, Thachin hmuh awkah, Midang zong hmuh awkah, Jesuh sin hal tuah.
