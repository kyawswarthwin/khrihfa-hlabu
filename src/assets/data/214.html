JESUH TUM THAN TIANG KAN TUAN LAI

Doh is F

1. Sifahnak in ruahchan ram tha, Zeitikdah ka phak lai? Zeitikah ralthuam ka chiah lai, Hnangamnak ka hmuh lai?
