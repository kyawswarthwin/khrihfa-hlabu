ZANLEI THANGTHATNAK

Doh is Ab

1. Tlang in ni a liam riangmang, Van nih lei kha a tongh cang, Van ah khin arfi hna cu, Mei inn bang an ceu lioah, Hngak law bia tuah.

CHO: Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Bawipa Pathian! Van le lei ah na khat dih, An in lawmh, Van le lei nih, Maw sang bik Bawi.

2. Nunnak Bawi van kulh chungah, Nangmah huhphenhnak inn ah Nangmah an kawlmi kan khawm, Na thladem tangin kan pom, Kan umpi ko.

CHO: Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Bawipa Pathian! Van le lei ah na khat dih, An in lawmh, Van le lei nih, Maw sang bik Bawi.

3. Muih thlam aa zelh lioah khan, Dawt thinlung nih a kulh dih, Sunpar arfi lengah khin, Na hmai hmuh kan duh ca-ah, Kan lung an zuang.

CHO: Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Bawipa Pathian! Van le lei ah na khat dih, An in lawmh, Van le lei nih, Maw sang bik Bawi.

4. Zan fikceu a liam tikah, Chun le zan an liam zongah, Vanmi Bawipa, na thawngin, Zing khuaceu a ra than i, Muihnak a dih.

CHO: Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Thiang bik, Bawipa Pathian! Van le lei ah na khat dih, An in lawmh, Van le lei nih, Maw sang bik Bawi.
