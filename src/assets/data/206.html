JESUH THI LAWNG IN

Doh is G

1. Ka sualnak zei in thiang lai, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko. Zeidah a ka damter lai? Jesuh thi lawngin ka dam lai.

CHO: Maw thletmi sung bik thi, Hawhra bang rantertu, Cerhti dang ka tong lo, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

2. Keimah thianter awk ca-ah, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko. Keimah sual ngaihthiam awkah, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

CHO: Maw thletmi sung bik thi, Hawhra bang rantertu, Cerhti dang ka tong lo, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

3. Keimah sual ca ingtu ah, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko. Atu tiang zei kaa hlei lo, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

CHO: Maw thletmi sung bik thi, Hawhra bang rantertu, Cerhti dang ka tong lo, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

4. Ka ruahchan lungnemnak ca, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko; Keimah miding sinak ah, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.

CHO: Maw thletmi sung bik thi, Hawhra bang rantertu, Cerhti dang ka tong lo, Jesuh thi lawngin a thiang ko.
