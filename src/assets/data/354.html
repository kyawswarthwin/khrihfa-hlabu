THA CHIN KHA REL TUAH

Doh is Eb

1. Vawleicung sifahnak kha na hmuh tikah, Na ruahchannak kha a dongh dengmang ahcun, Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat na rel lai i Pathian nih tampi an pek kha na hngalh lai.

CHO: Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat rel tuah, A tampi an pek kha na hngalh lai; Thachin rel tuah, pakhat hnu pakhat, Pathian nih zeitluk an dawt kha na hngalh lai.

2. Si tha mirum mi kha na hmuh tik hna ah, Jesuh nih pek an duhmi thil kha va ruat, Chaw in na cawk khawh lo na co awk thil kha, Vancung ah na hmuh lai mi kha, va ruat tuah.

CHO: Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat rel tuah, A tampi an pek kha na hngalh lai; Thachin rel tuah, pakhat hnu pakhat, Pathian nih zeitluk an dawt kha na hngalh lai.

3. Harnak le sifahnak na cung tlung hmanhseh, Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat va rel than tuah, Lungfehnak le thachin a si tiah ruat ko, Kannih thiannak ca harnak kha a herh ve.

CHO: Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat rel tuah, A tampi an pek kha na hngalh lai; Thachin rel tuah, pakhat hnu pakhat, Pathian nih zeitluk an dawt kha na hngalh lai.

4. Ngaih-chiatnak kha tlung hmanhseh, Zeihmanh va phang hlah Pathian nih laksawng tha nangmah ca a chiah. Khuadawm le nitlang vawlei ca a that bang Minung thatnak caah khuadawm kha a herh.

CHO: Thachin pakhat hnu pakhat rel tuah, A tampi an pek kha na hngalh lai; Thachin rel tuah, pakhat hnu pakhat, Pathian nih zeitluk an dawt kha na hngalh lai.
