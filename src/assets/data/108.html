KHUARUAHHAR KHAMHTU

Doh is D

1. Sualthlahnak kha Khrih nih a ser, Khuaruahhar ngaingai a si! Ka sualthlahnak man kha a pek, Khamhnak khuaruahhar a si!

CHO: Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Maw ka Jesuh, Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Ka Bawipa.

2. Ka lungchung sualnak a thianter, Khuaruahhar Khamhbawi a si! Keimah he a kal ti zungzal, Nifatin a kan zohkhenh!

CHO: Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Maw ka Jesuh, Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Ka Bawipa.

3. A thiang thi kha a thlet ca ah, Amah ka thangthat theo lai. Ka thlarau kha a dawt ca ah, Pathian sin a phakpi lai,

CHO: Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Maw ka Jesuh, Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Ka Bawipa.

4. Teinak thazang kha a ka pek, Khuaruahhar Khamhbawi a si, Ka lung Amah sin ka pek lai, Vawlei zeihmanh ruat loin.

CHO: Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Maw ka Jesuh, Khuaruahharnak kha Jesuh a si, Ka Bawipa.
