A AW KA HNGALH

Doh is Ab

1. Ka Khamhtu ka hmu lo na-in, Nifa ka sin-ah bia a chim, Ka Bawi ah ka thim cang ca-ah, Bawipa thangthat a aw ka hngalh.

CHO: A aw ka hngalh Ka Khamhtu aw, Lunglawmh thanuamh nak a ka pek, Bia a ka ruah tik-ah ka lawm, Bawipa thangthat, A aw ka hngalh.

2. A muisam ka hmu lo na-in, Thlichia lak-ah a aw ka theih, Tilet cu dai u, a ti hna, Ni a tlang i zeizong an dai.

CHO: A aw ka hngalh Ka Khamhtu aw, Lunglawmh thanuamh nak a ka pek, Bia a ka ruah tik-ah ka lawm, Bawipa thangthat, A aw ka hngalh.

3. Bia-tak le bia ding a ka chimn, Mit hmuh lo lawmhnak le dinhnak, Hna-ngam dawtnak kong a ka chimh, Vancung inn dawh kong a ka chimh.

CHO: A aw ka hngalh Ka Khamhtu aw, Lunglawmh thanuamh nak a ka pek, Bia a ka ruah tik-ah ka lawm, Bawipa thangthat, A aw ka hngalh.
