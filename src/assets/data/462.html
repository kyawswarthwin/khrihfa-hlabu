HALLELUJAH, MAW KA KHAMHTU

Doh is C

1. Pathian Fa a rungtum ah, "Ngaih-chiatnak tuarmi," an ti, Cucu khua-ruahhar a si, Hal-le-lu-jah, Maw ka Khamhtu!

2. Ningzah le nihsawh tuar in, Kei ca sualphawt in a dir, Thi in ka sual a ngaihthiam, Hal-le-lu-jah, Maw ka Khamhtu!

3. Pathian Tuufa thiang a si, Kannih cu misual kan si, Remnak a si kho hnga maw? Hal-le-lu-jah, Maw ka Khamhtu!

4. Thah awkah Jesuh an tar, "A dih cang," tiin a au, Van ah thangthat in a um, Hal-le-lu-jah, maw ka Khamhtu!

5. Kan Bawi sungbik rat tikah, Tlanhmi van in auh awkah, Hi hla kan sa than te lai, Hal-le-lu-jah, maw ka Khamhtu!
