FEK TEIN DIR U SI

Doh is G

1. Khrihfa ralkap mi hna, Bawi Jesuh bochan in, Thawngthabia a thiang bik aupi cio u, Raldohnak le ngaihchiatnak, Rili bang lian ko hmanhseh, Jesuh thawngin hodah a kan tei hnga.

CHO: Fek te-in dir u si, Teinak tiang kal chin lengmang u si, Bawi bik ralkap tha kan si, Bia a kan kam bantukin, Teinak sui luchin kan hmuh cio lai.

2. Jesuh Khrih bochan in, Rulhreu ral do u si, Nan ngun-nam hler u law, hmaiah kal u, Bawi Jesuh nih, nan hmaiah ka dir zungzal lai, a ti, A bia kam bantuk in kan tei theo lai.

3. Vawlei ngaihchiatnak le, Sifah kan lonh tikah, Vancung khua ah kan hawidawtmi hna he, Dong lo-in Pathian thangthatnak hla kan sa zungzal lai, Ngaihchiat sifah retheih um ti lai lo.
