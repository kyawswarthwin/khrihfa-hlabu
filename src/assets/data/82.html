NOEL HMASA BIK

Doh is D

1. Noel hmasa bik a chuahnak cu, Vanmi nih tlimno rawn i tuukhal an chimh hna, Tlimno rawn i tuu an zoh lioah, Cu khuasik khuamawt le zantim ah cun.

CHO: Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Israel Siangpahrang a chuak.

2. Van an zoh, arfi pakhat an hmuh: Nichuah nak lei lam hlapi ah khin a ceu i, Vawlei cung ah fak piin a ceu, Chun le zan hlan in a ceu cam cin ko.

CHO: Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Israel Siangpahrang a chuak.

3. Cu arfi ceunak zoh in mifim, Pathum nichuahnak lei lamhlapi in an ra, Siangpahrang pakhat kawl an tum i, Arfi kha khuazeika hmanh ah an zulh.

CHO: Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Israel Siangpahrang a chuak.

4. Nitlaklei ah hi arfi aa thawn, Bethlehem khua cungah mi chimh bang aa din ko, Khika khin a tang i a um colh; Bawi Jesuh Khrih ihnak cung zawn te ah.

CHO: Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Israel Siangpahrang a chuak.

5. Mifim pathum hna cu an vung lut, Hmaizahnak le upatnak in an khuk an bil, An laksawng sung bik kha i put in, An sui le marh le frankinsens an pek.

CHO: Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Israel Siangpahrang a chuak.
