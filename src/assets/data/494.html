INN NUAM, INN THLUACHUAK

Doh is C

1. Bawi Khrih umnak inn cu inn nuam a si, Dawtnak, remnak zungzal in a leng, Hi innchungkhar thluachuah na pekmi nih na min sunhlawinak a chuahpi tawn.

2. Bawi Khrih ukmi inn cu inn nuam a si, Satan hlennak nih a hrawk kho lo, Khrihfabu caah hrih le hram a si i zohchunh awk ca zongah a si.
