ZOH LAW NUNG

Doh is G

1. Bawipa sinin thawng ka theih, Hal-le-lu-jah, Cu thawngtha cu nang kan thanh lai; A biathiang chungah a um Hal-le-lu-jah, Zoh law nung tuah ti bia thiang a si.

CHO: Maw unau, zoh law nung tuah, Jesuh zoh law na nung lai, A bia thiang chungah a um. Hal-le-lu-jah, Zoh law nung tuah ti bia thiang a si.

2. A dawtnak thawngtha ka ngei, Hal-le-lu-jah, Nangmah ca ah thanhmi thawngtha; Cunglei in thanhmi thawngtha Hal-le-lu-jah, Jesuh chimmi biatak cu ka hngalh.

CHO: Maw unau, zoh law nung tuah, Jesuh zoh law na nung lai, A bia thiang chungah a um. Hal-le-lu-jah, Zoh law nung tuah ti bia thiang a si.

3. Nunnak cu pek na si cang, Hal-le-lu-jah, Zungzal nunnak cu na ngei lai; Amah lawng kha va zoh tuah, Hal-le-lu-jah, Jesuh lawng khamh khotu asi.

CHO: Maw unau, zoh law nung tuah, Jesuh zoh law na nung lai, A bia thiang chungah a um. Hal-le-lu-jah, Zoh law nung tuah ti bia thiang a si.
