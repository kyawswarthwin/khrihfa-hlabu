VAWLEI KHUAZAKIP AH JESUH HE

Doh is D

1. Vawlei khua zakip ah, Ka Bawi Jesuh he, Keimah cu him tein ka kal zungzal ko, Amah loin khuazei hmanh ah kaa lawm lo, A um ahcun khua zeihmanh ah ka tih lo.

CHO: Khua zeihmanh ah, Jesuh thawngin ka him ko. Khuazei hmanh, Khua zeihmanh, Zeihmanh ka phang lo.

2. Jesuh a um tikah ka van a sang lo, Ka kawi a ka hlaw nain Jesuh um ko, A harbik lam chungah khan, Phan hmanh ning law, Jesuh he ka um ahcun zungzal kaa lawm.

CHO: Khua zeihmanh ah, Jesuh thawngin ka him ko. Khuazei hmanh, Khua zeihmanh, Zeihmanh ka phang lo.

3. Khua zeihmanh ah thihnak caan kha cu ning law, Vancung khua ah, Jesuh a ka kalpi lai, Vawlei lam har chungah ka chuak than lai lo, Khuazei hmanh ah Jesuh he ka hna ngam ko.

CHO: Khua zeihmanh ah, Jesuh thawngin ka him ko. Khuazei hmanh, Khua zeihmanh, Zeihmanh ka phang lo.
