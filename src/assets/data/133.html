KHRIH VAILAM AH

Doh is C

1. Khrih vailam kei chawrnak a si, Caan hlun chungin ka hmun ko, A thiang tuanbia ceunak vialte, Vailam parah an i pum.

2. Ngaihchiatnak a ka tlunh tikah, Ruahchan a lo, tih a zual, Vailam nih a ka hlaw lai lo, Zohhmanh, daihnak he a ceu.

3. Lunglawmhnak ni a tlan tikah, Ka lam ceu dawtnak a um, Vailam ceu thlam a chuakmi nih, Ni ceu kha a dawhter chin.

4. Thluachuah vanluh, sifah retheih, Vailam nih a thianter ko; Khingthlai khawh lo daihnak a um, A hmun zungzal lawmhnak he.
