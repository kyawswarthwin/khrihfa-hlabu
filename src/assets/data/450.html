BEAULAH RAM

Doh is G

1. Facang le mitsur he a khat, Keimah roconak khua ka phak, Khi khuachung cu zungzal a ceu, Zan muihnak zong a um ti lo.

CHO: Maw Beaulah ram, Maw ram sung bik, Tlangcung par in ka dir tikah Kei ca a remh inn tha ka hmuh, Rili khatte-lei kam ah khin, Ceunak ram kha keimah ka hmuh, Kei ca zungzal umnak a si.

2. Khamhbawi nih keimah he aa chok, Dawtnak in kannih kan i chawn A kut in lam a ka hruai i, Vancung hawi-komhnak tha a si.

3. Zungzal a hring thing umnak in, Rim hmuinak kha thli nih a hranh, Uai lo pangpar zungzal a kho, Nunnak cerhti zungzal a luang.
