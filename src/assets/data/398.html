JESUH KA CHIMH LAI

Doh is Eb

1. Ka harnak vialte Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ka thilrit kei lawng ka phor kho lo, Ka lung retheih lioah a ka bawmh lai, A ka dawt i a ka ruah zungzal.

CHO: Jesuh ka chimh lai, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ka thilrit kei lawng ka phor kho lo. Jesuh ka chimh lai! Jesuh ka chimh lai Jesuh lawng bawm kho tu cu a si.

2. Ka temh innak cu Jesuh ka chimh lai, Amah cu zawn ruah nak he a khat, Bawmhnak ka hal ah cun a ka khamh lai, Ka temh innak cu a donghter lai.

CHO: Jesuh ka chimh lai, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ka thilrit kei lawng ka phor kho lo. Jesuh ka chimh lai! Jesuh ka chimh lai Jesuh lawng bawm kho tu cu a si.

3. Hneksaknak chungin Khamhu Bawi ka herh, Ka thilrit vialte chawng kho tu cu, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ngaihchiatnak vialte a ka chan lai.

CHO: Jesuh ka chimh lai, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ka thilrit kei lawng ka phor kho lo. Jesuh ka chimh lai! Jesuh ka chimh lai Jesuh lawng bawm kho tu cu a si.

4. Vawlei nuamhnak nih an ka lem lengmang, Sualnak tuah dingin an ka lem tawn Jesuh ka chimh lai i, a ka hruai lai, Vawlei vialte ka tei khawh nakhnga.

CHO: Jesuh ka chimh lai, Jesuh ka chimh lai, Ka thilrit kei lawng ka phor kho lo. Jesuh ka chimh lai! Jesuh ka chimh lai Jesuh lawng bawm kho tu cu a si.
