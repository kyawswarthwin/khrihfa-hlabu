KALVARY AH KA HRUAI

Doh is Eb

1. Ka nunnak Bawi kan thangthat lai, Sunpar nang ca si seh, Na hling luchin ka philh sual lai. Kalvary ah ka hruai.

CHO: Gethsemani na thlacamnak, Na tahhramnak vialte he khan, Na dawtnak ka philh nakhnga lo, Kalvary ah ka hruai.

2. An in vuinak thlan ka hmuhsak, Lunghno in an in tah, Ceunak puan thuamh in vanmi nih, Nangmah kha an in ven.

CHO: Gethsemani na thlacamnak, Na tahhramnak vialte he khan, Na dawtnak ka philh nakhnga lo, Kalvary ah ka hruai.

3. Meri bang zingah laksawng he, Na sin ka ratter tuah, Na thlan lawngmi kha ka hmuhsak, Kalvary ah ka hruai.

CHO: Gethsemani na thlacamnak, Na tahhramnak vialte he khan, Na dawtnak ka philh nakhnga lo, Kalvary ah ka hruai.

4. Bawipa, nang ca nifa vailam, Put awk ka lung a si, Na ngaihchiatnak in ve ka duh, Kei ca na in dih cang.

CHO: Gethsemani na thlacamnak, Na tahhramnak vialte he khan, Na dawtnak ka philh nakhnga lo, Kalvary ah ka hruai.
