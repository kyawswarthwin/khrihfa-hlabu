JESUH NIH A PEN ZUNGZAL

Doh is E

1. Ni le thla a kal tawnnak lam, Jesuh a um a pen zungzal, A ngaite vawlei a deng tiang, A pennak a kau chinchin lai.

2. Holh phun dang hmangmi vialte nih, A dawtnak kha an thangthat cio, Ngakchia zong nih a vel-ngeihnak, Aw thlum tein hla kha an phuah.

3. A-mah sinah dong hin lo in, Thangthat le thlacam an kai ti; A min cu zi-hmui rimthaw bang, Zing fatein a kai zungzal.

4. Hi vawlei cung mi vialte nih, Kan Siangpahrang thangthat tuah u, Vancungmi hna tum than tuah u, Nan aw thlum in hla sa ve u.

5. Khua zakip kha Jesuh a uk, Thatnak a tam a karh chin ko; Sifakmi le re-theimi nih, A zungzal in an i lawm lai.
