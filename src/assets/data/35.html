KA NUNNAK, BAWIPA CU THANGTHAT!

Doh is Bb

1. Bawipa thangthat tuah u, A ngan bik Pathian, Kannih khamhnak ca ah lam tha kha a hun, Khamhnak a tha bik kha a kan pek ca ah, Mei alh le minmei in a kan kaipi lai.

CHO: Nan... aw ngeih vialte in, Bawipa thangthat u, Mi lungchung muihnak kha a run ceuter lai, Amah pen dinnak kha a kau chinchin lai, A sermi vial-te nih, A sermi vial-te nih Bawipa an hlorh, Maw ka thlarau thangthat tuah.

2. Thlarau tlaumi sinah, Tlanhnak kha a pek, A sualmi ca thiannak tiva a luanter, A khamh ciami vialte kha a kan dawt i, Kan thlacam a ngaih ca, Bawipa thangthat u.

CHO: Nan... aw ngeih vialte in, Bawipa thangthat u, Mi lungchung muihnak kha a run ceuter lai, Amah pen dinnak kha a kau chinchin lai, A sermi vial-te nih, A sermi vial-te nih Bawipa an hlorh, Maw ka thlarau thangthat tuah.

3. Bawipa thangthat tuah u, Kan vansan lioah, A zaangfahnak hri nih khan a kan tlaih cang, Zumh le ruahchan in a teimi vialte he, Dawtnak ram ah tling tein kan i tong lai.
