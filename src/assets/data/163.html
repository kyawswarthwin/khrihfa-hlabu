JESUH A TUM THAN LAI

Doh is D

1. Khuadeinak arfi deirel chuah caan ah siseh, Khuamuihnak ka in ni a chuah lioah siseh, Jesuh lianhnak he hi vawlei ah a tum lai, A zummi a khawmh hna lai.

CHO: Maw ka Pathian, zeitikah dah lawmhnak hla ka sak lai? Hal-le-lu-jah! Jesuh tum than, Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen! Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen!

2. Chun ling ah siseh, zanlei sang zongah siseh, Zantim khuamui, mui cirik caan khum-ah siseh, Pathian lianhngannak in Jesuh kha a tum lai, A zummi a khawmh hna lai.

CHO: Maw ka Pathian, zeitikah dah lawmhnak hla ka sak lai? Hal-le-lu-jah! Jesuh tum than, Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen! Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen!

3. Mithiangmi hna le, vancungmi cu hmunkhat ah, An thawng a thang lai, vancung in a tum lioah, Pathian lianhnak kha, A velchum ah a tlang lai, A zummi a khawmh hna lai.

CHO: Maw ka Pathian, zeitikah dah lawmhnak hla ka sak lai? Hal-le-lu-jah! Jesuh tum than, Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen! Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen!

4. Kan lung tum bik cu, phak kan duh ko thih loin, Ngaihchiat, tahhram, zawt, retheih, sifah luatnak in, Pathian nih minmei in a kan kaipi ahcun, Zeitluk in kan i lawmh lai.

CHO: Maw ka Pathian, zeitikah dah lawmhnak hla ka sak lai? Hal-le-lu-jah! Jesuh tum than, Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen! Hal-le-lu-jah! Amen!
