PATHIAN RIAN CA, KUT TAM A HERH

Doh is G

1. Pathian, na rian-tuannak ah hin, Kut tampi a herh ko, Kannih tel kan duh ve ca-ah, Kan laksawng kan rak put.

2. Bawipa, nangmah kan in dawt i, Na rian kan duh ca-ah, Kan laksawng kha kan rak put i, Na kehram ah kan chiah.

3. Bawipa, kan laksawng tel lo hin, A tuan kho lo an um, Jesuh min in kan cohlang law, Na duh bangin hmang ko.
